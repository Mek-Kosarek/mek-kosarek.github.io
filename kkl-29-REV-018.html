As Babel loba taogha
18
1 Neneko uro yabioba, nari haing uamna kwelekamna senenag agha ililamnoba, Allah arukna malaikat yogne imag agha kulukul alamoba imno. Eldi saelba uhirop mikipne nubu wamoba, elag imang wamonge nenekori mog so'o tibin warelobo. 2 Yubu nubunge agha lelamori,
“Wene loba tal! As kaluro sorop as Babel nubunge eneko tapto loba tal!
Wene as enekoag phia tognol tanag taori,
mema yongeri nimi nepsiropne ua urobag wamla.
As lomag tao wamnebagna, ni winang nimiri winang sop tehirop kom winangap,
nimiri mali senehirop winangap wamnebag agha tao.
3 As kaluro sorop as Babel nimiri samen malia ulamong nena,
kelabori nimi sae kiliro mag yonge tebatsiro nenek nenelamsilori,
wana tohiro nengaboap salik-malik waleptangka malia ulamloa saog uro ulamongpa,
tala-tala ni mog so'oag nimi Babel nimiag neleplamsiong.
Mog so'oag nimi sembe yubu sia lennangdi,
Nuni Imagne poneko wailag nembi agha
kelaboap salik-malik waleptangkamlonga saog uro as nubunge nenekoap ma'aptangkamong.
Kamna kapto toltoro tolongorop nang mog so'oag tala-tala wamong nang as enekoag pang-yang ulamongdi,
nimiri wanaag, ‘Nanag uro yaboho,’ senenne wali-waliaba along kamna kapto toro palamongdi, tup talamong.
Salik-malik walep tangtop kel enekoag nelebom agha Nuni Allah wailag nembaong ane sembe, as soro kalurop as Babel nubunge eneko wene tapto loba tal!”
aro lelamoa ka'ebano.
4 Nen nari ka'elamnoag agha Imagneri yubu nhon lelamongena,
“Lambahut! Nari mabo, a'undi as kaluro sorop as Babel tanekoa a'un lambalulom!
As aneko nimiri malia ulamangeag nelebahom tanena,
sinag nimi ohinne sekleroba tam timbanepne a'unag babe taleag aro lambalulom.
5 Malia ulamangdi, sembenga loloro palilamlange neneko ere-ere waelbaongdi,
imag tai yangkalamloa saogne as aneko nimiri malia ulamangne sembe Allah el wamla.
6 Babel enekori nimiag tarelamsiog saog uro elag lunuro tatlulom.
Eldi nimiag yonge ulamsiogne nena,
eldi mag yonge tebatsiro nenek nenelamsiloa saog uro ulamogne nene sembe
a'undi lunulapmundi, nen phelekto elag ma'aro yonge piplulom.
7 Kel enekori eldangkel wanaag paliag neneldalamlari,
‘Yauog kel wamna komdi, na yubu sia lerop kel nubunge wamna.
Ik nhon babe amang uro yaghe sembanun kom,’ alamla.
‘Bobonge agto yaboho,’ senenne agha tup nenel dalamog.
Ane sembe, wene wali-walia maikno elag to'opto wamlange talup lambamundi,
seklenne agha to'opto lumbamun andarina, wana ikin uro seklebalul.
Eldi wanaag, ‘Ik nhon babe amang uro yaghe sembanun kom,’ alamlange sembe,
8 ik nhon sum agha nimi ohinne elag omnep nena,
tepnange babe, amang uro unne babe, kwaneng yo seklenne babe
elag uro yabilul.
Nuni Allah nu Saelbamsilne mikip sirik wamlari,
kel eneko sembe tam lelekto kemero kareplamleba,
ik nhon sum agha elna aukti tero kom talul,”
aro wene alamoa ka'ebano.
9 Wene aro ka'ebanongena, as kaluro sorop as Babel eneko aukti telamleri, auk mhong kuka walelamleba, mog so'oag nimi sembe yubu sia lerop nangdi ililamikti, ngolol aro engelamukang. Mog so'oag nimi sembe yubu sia lennangdi, “Bobonge nanag wamlange agto yaboho,” aro ulamongdi, kelaboap salik-malik waleptankamloa saog uro Nuni Imagne poneko wailag nembi agha as kaluro sorop as nenekoap ma'aptangkamong. Ane sembe, as kaluro sorop as neneko aukti telamleba, yubu sia lerop nangdi engero, ‘Wali-waliaba nen nunag talul kom,’ aro ngolol alamukang. 10 As Babel aneko auk telamleba, seklelamukangne sembe yubu sia lerop nang ‘Nunag taleag,’ aro log lelem weag sekamikti, tiboghamukang. Sin weag sekamikti lelamikti,
‘Yaghe, seklelamang-e! As Babel kaluro sorop as nubunge eneko
as mikip sirikne wamo aghana, ot heng ko'o nhonoghag agha
tam pelengeloglaba, loba tal!” alamukang.
11 Nimiri kamna kapto toltoro tolongorop nimi mog so'oag tala-tala wamong nimi tanekori babe, as nubunge neneko sembe amang pairo engelamukang nena, “As eneko wamso tanena, sikindinge taneko kamna kapto tolaheng,” senelamukang aghana, kom taleba, “Nimi nhondi babe nuri saeag payalamapne kamna kapto tolaukang kom,” aro engelamukang. 12 As nubunge neneko anam aukti ten komag, nimiri wana awi sirik uro kamna alikinag uro kapto tomnep nena,
leng-leng aroba emasap,
leng-leng aroba perakap,
kirik wana yame-yame upsererop kirik wana elektobaap,
wali sik wana saogne mutiaraap,
ag wet purum winne agha wirop ag walingeap, wet purum yongonne agha wirop ag walingeap,
ag walinge uwongneap, uwong ag agha poptangtopneap,
kal ken-ken song wali saog kalap,
pham nubu sirik pham gadya sombola wenge agha sunurobaap,
kal kamna alikinag urop kal agha sunurobaap,
besi agha sunuroba uwongneap piringeap,
ae sorop kirik sildi yogto-yogto pairop kirikap,
13 kwaneng ma'al yona emem arobaap,
emem aroba kal konap,
song walia aukag tamnepneap,
nimi nongag likto song wali nenerobaap,
kal wea song walingeap,
haing anggur wana loltero terop maghap,
kal zaitun wana lolterop maghap,
kwaneng roti sunuroba leng-lenga walingeap,
kwaneng roti sunuroba gandum walingeap,
pham sapiap, pham dombaap,
pham kudaap, nimi alibag pukamnaba pham kudari kiliro pabihiropneap,
nimi babe, arukna nembahinep nimi nong kamna kapto pabihirop nimiap,
anam as nubunge Babel eneko aukti ten komag, pibom agha nimiri kamna kapto tol pinep.
14 Kamna kapto toro tolongorop nimi tanekori as nubunge nenekoag ikinne timbalenge iogha lelamikti, “Andi wanaag ‘Wali-walia topsene,’ senelamlamne neneko ni kom tal. An tuptangka an holno seneba ni ka'eseptaoba, nia taneko nen iplulam kom,” alamukang. 15 As kaluro sorop as neneko loba talamleba, ogti lebareklamlenge sembe, sin samenag as enekoag nimiag kamna kapto toroaekti, tup taek nimi taneko weag agha tiboghaukang. Tiboghaikti log lelem weag sekamikti, amang pairo engelamukang. 16 Engelamikti yubu lelamikti,
“Yaghe, seklelamang-e! As kaluro sorop as nubunge Babel eneko mane agha loba tal!
Elag kwiria elektoba enekamongena, wet purum winne agha wirop agap,
ag uwong popneap, uwong agap eneka urop.
Eldi sop-sop towalengeptangtobana leng-leng aroba emasap,
kamna alikinag agha sik wana saog uropne mutiaraap,
kirik wana yame-yame upsererop kirikap towalengeptangka urop.
17 Aghana heng ko'o nhonoghag agha elag tup ua ulamlange neneko orog tal,” alamukang.
Mag alip toman kabong nubunge alibag pirop nimi ni, kabong o'orop nang sikini yaboap, kabong lomag o'orop nangap, “Mag lomag uroba sembe ero toroaukap,” aro kabongag nhon pirop nangap, sin niri weag agha sekamikti tiboghamukang.
18 Weag sekamikti tilamikpa, as eneko auk telamleri, mhong kuklamlea tiboghamukang. Tiboghamikti yubu haum alamikti, “As nubunge nhon as ene saog uro nubu uro soro kaulbaekne nhon urop kom,” alamukang. 19 Unulamlea sembe sin engelamikti, amang uropne agha usoghag hao lopto towalengel dalamikti, yubu engenange agha nubu uro engelamukang. Engelamikti,
“Yaghe, seklelamang-e! As soro kalurop as nubunge Babel eneko mane agha loba tal!
As eneag tup wamlangeri nu kabong mag alip toman piroba ngai nimi tup nenelamsil!
Aghana heng ko'o nhonoghag agha nia-ni wamlange loba tal,”
alamukang.
20 A'un imag ua urop nang, as kaluro sorop as neneko kom talne sembe yeng uro wamsut!
A'un Allah Elmabo wepsirop nimiap, A'un Yesusti wepto pogsirop nangap, a'un Allah yubu silimu tiptop nangap, awi senelamsut!
As neneko nimiri a'unag malia uhiropne sembe Allahri tam pelengero lumbahil!
21 Neneko uro yabioba, imnori kemelamnoag agha Allah arukna malaikat mikipne nhondi kirik nubu sirikne saelbamori, mag laut longorop mag nubungeag tobog aori, yubu lelamori,
“Undo kirik nene mikip uro pelengero toboghan saog uro,
as Babel soro kaubaogne neneko yo uro wa'epto toboghaleba,
nimi nhondi babe olog ibukang kom.
22 Pingpong yubu neneroba kecapi orop nimiri olamika lilim babe,
sepna septop nimiri seplamika babe,
ma'i agha lom nembongkia pho alamikpa yubu lelamlea babe,
ma'i trompet pho alamika babe an as Babel tanekoag nimiri nen ka'ebaukang kom.
Sa'a-sa'a ni kop-kop tagto el wamnari, sunuro sorop nimi babe as tanekoag ibukang kom.
Kwaneng roti koloptop wana ururo yambanep kirikti yubu lelamlea babe as tanekoag ka'ebaukang kom.
23 As tanekoag whea nhon babe nen wareamlul kom.
Anekoa nimiri kelabo tomnep sepna septo irit pholong alamika babe ka'ebaukang kom.
Samen as soro kalurop as Babel neneag agha
kamna kapto toltoro tolongorop nimi tup nang nubu sirik nang nenep taong.
An Babel nimiri mili lero nimi mog so'oag nimi ulamsiloa saog ulamomba,
mog so'oag ni tala-tala wamong nimiri a'unag, ‘Nubunang,’ aro nelebahiongdi,
orolena sik senelamong,”
aro Allah arukna malaikat ponekori alamoa ka'ebano.
24 As Babel nimi tanekori mog so'oag ni tala-tala wamong nimi opsiongba,
Allah yubu silimu tiptop nangap, Allah Elmabo weptop nimiap eneng lanaro tebaong.
