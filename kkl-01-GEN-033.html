Yakub Esauap haiptareka (33:1-20)
33
1 Palamekti, Yakubti tilamogpa, Esau eldi arukna nimi teng naual tarabareap (400) yalamek. Yalamekpa, tibogahiogti, Yakubti mabo ni tane wamekne pogto, nenne Leaag nembahiogti, nenne Rahelag nembahiogti, nenne eldi arukna phende kelabo abenekoag nembahiog. 2 Eldi arukna phende kelabo abeneko sikin maboap usoghag nembahiogti, Lea elmaboap nusamag nembahiogti, Rahel elme Yusupap amik nembahiogpa, palamek. 3 Yakub samenag menangkaogti, yabu sanukto pulingkiro palamogti, ik saek bare yabu sanukto pulingkiogti, eldo peramag piog. 4 Eldo peramag piogpa, eldori, “Naipsae,” aro merea somoro salel piogpa, kiptangto engeltalamdek.
5 Esauri Yakubti kelabo me kembahiogti, “Tane et nimi?” aro haibaogpa, Yakubti, “Tanena nari kelabo mabo. Allah wana waliri tatneonge,” seog.
6 Wene seogpa, eldi arukna eldi kelabo nembahiogne abeneko sikin maboap Esau whingag yabu sanukto pulingkiek. 7 Ane Lea elmaboap Esau whingag yabu sanukto pulingkiek. Ane Yusup ilin Rahelap Esau whingag yabu sanukto pulingkirek.
8 Pulingkiekpa Esauri yubu lelamogti, “Wela sembalamdi, nimiap phamap pogsilamne haiptaapne nene?” seogpa Yakubti, “Naro, andi o'ona sembahilulam,” aro seog.
9 Wene seogpa, eldo Esauri Yakubag, “Naipsae, nanag sae agha maikno wamla. Andi na nen tatneheng kom,” seog.
10 Wene seogpa Yakubti, “Undo kom. Andi o'ona sembanelamba, nari saeag agha tatkenne toro alulam. Haiptanamdi, andi whing kembaghendi, Allahri whing iplo saog uro an naro nari ipken. Ane sembe na wana wali,” seog. 11 Seogti, “Nari payandi, tatkenne nene nu kiptangto sembe tatkendi, toro alulam. Allah wana waliri nanag kwema senelamnonge maikno tatneo, ane sembe lipseng kom,” aro mikip lelamogha sembe tobog.
12 Wene alamogpa, tarogha taneko tobogti, yubu lelamogti, “Wene nhon togheberi, nhon palamukap,” seog.
13-14 Wene seog, aghana Yakubti lelamogti, “Naro, el wamlam, me tanena elemag pinep kom saog, apna pham me maum telamangneap. Ik nhon sum elemag pana babe pham tane tebanepti, an samenag palapmenba, na amik nemen-nemen meap phamap yan nginikom agha palamika sunsunum uro Seir mog so'o pina wapmenag yanun,” seog.
15 Wene seogpa Esauri, “Wene selam, tanena nari arukna me etpare undo piloa pihinun,” seogpa, Yakubti, “Mo palamebea na sembe ikin sembaheng kom. Naro, na sembe awi senelamnelamba, ot an sembe na yeng,” seog.
16 Wene seogti, Esau sumeneko sum elsam Edom piog. 17 Esau Edom palamogpa, Yakub Sukot piogti, ae eldamne sembe soro eldi pham tanekoap sembe babe kalun ae sobaog. Ane sembe mog so'o si ane Sukota engerop.
18 Yakub Aram so'oag agha Kanaan so'oag as Sikhem piogha, wali uro piogti, as Sikhem peramag ae soro wamog. 19 Ae soro wamoghag aneko mog so'o aneko Hamor sisa nimiringe. Sikhem poneko babe Hamor elme wamog. Yakubti, “Mog so'o wene wamnaag aneko naringe ya'ag wamlul,” aro kamna kapto kirik wana solomne teng tombare (100) agha kamna kapto tobog. 20 Toboghag aneko Allah sembe si lero mhong towalengenag sobaogti, si “El Elohe Israel”b engkaog.
