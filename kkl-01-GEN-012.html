Allahri Abram yobogha (12:1-9)
12
1 Wamekpa Nuni Allahri Abramag yubu lelamogti, “Anenge asap, ankaboap, anenge so'oap laplobi agha nari mog so'o ambatkenenge nhonag pululam,” seogti, 2 “Andi mangarobari mangkahyikne tanekori mangkahyikne maikno talamikti, ‘Nimi sisa nubunge wamle,’ aro mikip nembahineba an si paliag wamleba an pere agha nimi maikno Nari walingena tatsinun. 3 Nimiri an yeplangkiikne Nari sin yepsinun. Nimiri an walangkiikne nari sin wabahinun. An pere agha nimi mog so'oag tala-tala wamikne ni sembe walingena tatsinun,” seog. 4-5 Abram mangkaekne hengkun taibuka taubuka ulamogha teng phende epto saek tarabare (75) wamogpa Nuni Allahri ambarogne sunsunum uro, “Kanaan so'oag pukap,” aro as Kharan laplobi agha palamekti elkel Saraiap, ilipsari me Lotap, eldi aruknap, sikindinge sae agha wamoghap toro aekti palamek. 6 Palameka Kanaan so'oag piekti Sikhem peramag kal nubu kal bari tamag Morea so'oag piek. Piekag aneko Kanaan nimi wamekag piek. 7 Wamekpa Nuni Allah poneko Abram whingag lag taogti, “Mog so'o nene anag agha likto pilulneag tatsinun,” aro ambarog. Ambarogpa Abram nenekori, “Nani Allah na whingag lag talne sembe mhong towalengkimnun,” aro kirik yangaro pibog. 8 Kirik yangaro pibogti, “Mag kwasag sip pinun,” aro piogti heng walelingkirobag as Ai anabi as Betel heng purukul pirobag anabiag ae kalun ae soro wamog. Aneko wamogti, “Nuni Allahag molona molbanun,” aro mhong towalengenag nhon yangaro sobaog. 9 Wameka nen lamba palamekti Negebb so'o mektopneag kulukul palamek.
Abram Mesir piogha (12:10-20)
10 Negeb so'oag aneko pina wamekpa, kwaneng yo nubunge wamogpa, “Kwaneng erarukti Mesir pukap,” aro palamek. 11 As Mesir peramag piekti elkel Sarai ambarelamogti, “An saren haing elel wamlamba 12 Mesir nimiri an kembagheikti, “Kel ene Abram elkel wamla,” aro seneraghikti, an lililangkeikti na omnukang. 13 Ane sembe andi, ‘Na Abram ilipsa'ael wamna,’ semenba sindi an sembe na omnukang kom. Sindi an sembe, ‘Ilipsa'ael wamla,’ aro nanag walia ulamukang,” aro ambarog. 14 “Walia ulamukang,” aro Sarai ambarogpa, Mesir piekpa Mesir nimiri kemelamekpa Sarai saren kemerop wamog. 15 Mesir nimi sembe yubu sia leropne Firaunc wamog. Firaundi yubu eptop nang tanekori babe ibekti, “Saren walinge yal,” aro sikini ambarel piek. Ambarel piekti Sarai poa Firaundi aeag pelepto payaek. 16 Payaekpa Abram sembe, “Sarai eldo wamla,” aro Firaundi walia Abramag ulamogti Firaun eldamnang arukna-ap, pham domba, kambing, sapiap, popsirop pham keledai, untaap pogto tatsiog. 17 Firaundi Abramdi kel tobogne sembe Nuni Allahri Firaundi ae lomap, ilipsaboap sembe naikna nubunge Allahri elag pibog. 18 Naikna nubunge pibogpa Firaundi Abram yobogti, “Andi sa'a sembe, ‘Sarai nari kel,’ sen koma orolenange ambatneom? 19 Sa'a sembe, ‘Naipsa'ael,’ aro orolena lebomba nari tomno? An kel ane wamlaba toro agha pululam,” seog. 20 Seogti, “Abram elkelap elenge aba ni toro agha as eneag agha lambaukangpa enero pabhululom,” aro Firaundi eldi arukna tanekoag arukahiog.
