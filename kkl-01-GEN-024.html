Abraham elme Ishak nenekori kelabo toro aogha (24:1-67)
24
1 Abrahamdi so'o as Hebron toboghag aneko ngai taogti, wamogpa, NUNI Allahri elag walia ulamog paghabog, nong sabal taog. 2 Sabal taogti ik nhon sum eldi arukna elenge aba ni paia ulamsiogne ponekoag yubu ambarelamogti, “Name, Andi sik uro ambatnululamne el tanundi, na yan kwagha mombolag sae tobongkiplulam. 3 Tobongkipmendi NUNI Allah Imagne im so'o saelbamlange nenekori ka'eamleba, name Ishak sembe andi, ‘Kanaan so'o eneag wamang kelabo ebanun kom,’ aro yubu sik uro leplulam. 4 Na mangkaneekag Ur-Kasdim pimendi, na nangkabo naipsabo sikin mangkelabo nhon el pululam,” aro ambarog.
5 Wene aro ambarogpa, eldi arukna anekori Abrahamag yubu lelamogti, “Kelabo Ishakti toplul sembanenge nenekori, ‘Anap Kanaan pinunam kom,’ sele tanena anme Ishak eneko enel aneba, andi laploa yalumag pinunam te?” aro haibaog. 6 Haibaogpa Abrahamdi, “Kom, na name tane wamlul. Enero pabululam kom! 7 NUNI Allah imag wamlange nenekori na naniri asag agha poa lambaneog. Poa lambaneogpa, nangkabo laplobi agha lambahiba, Allahri, ‘So'o Kanaan ane otsik anag agha likto pilengeag tatsinun,’ aro salag pibog. El nenekori, ‘An yepkulul,’ aro El Ilipsae malaikat poghol aleba, andi na name Ishak sembe kelabo aneko enero poa yalulam. 8 Kelabo anekori, ‘Anap Kanaan so'oag pinunam kom,’ sele tanena wene salag palilamnange sembe seneragna koma wana keyagman uro an mo yalulam. Ane sembe name enero poa pululam kom!” aro ambarog.
9 Wene aro ambarogpa, eldi arukna anekori ili Abrahamdi kwagha mombolag sae tobongkibogti, Abrahamdi lebogne sunsunum uro salag pibog. 10 Salag pibogti, eldi arukna anekori wali-walia iliringe tobogti, popsirop pham unta sa'obareag towalengkaogti, Abraham ilipsae Nahor samen wamoghag Aram mog so'o mag luag sip piog. 11 Piogti ae sinag kelabo mag tel alamekag, “Pham unta taneko tektek eplamik,” aro mag sembe so'o wailbaekag aneko tektek eplamog. 12 Tektek epto wamekpa, Abrahamdi arukna aneko Allahag yubu lero mololamogti, “Na nani Abraham Ili Allah wamlamne, Nani Abraham sembe o'ona sembame. Nani Abrahamdi sembao saog uro uanundi yepnululam! 13 Na wene mag waliroba peramag sekamna. Olog wamneba as eneag kelmabo mag telaukang. 14 Sin yaikne taneko nhon elag mololamneri, ‘Mag ma'iag teropne na mag olog tunundi,’ seneba, eldi, ‘Nani an temenba, anenge pham unta babe tebatsunun,’ selenge neneko sembe, ‘NUNI Allahri Ishak sembe kelabo nene weplange ane,’ sembanun. Nari pham unta babe mag tebatsile tanena, ‘Nani Abraham Allahri o'ona sembamlari, eldi seneragtopne sunsunum uro ual,’ aro el tan unulul,” aro Allahag molona molorop yubu lebog.
15-16 Allahag yubu molona lelamog abeneko kel saren nubu walinge nhon si Ribka mag ma'i teroba mag tebogti, walel alamog. Ribka neneko Betuel elmangkel. Betuel poneko Abraham ilipsae Nahor elme Milkari mangaro aogne. Ribka nengabo tomna koma wamogti yaog. Mag sembe wailbaekag aneko kulukaogti, mag ma'iag tebogti walel alamogpa, 17 Abraham arukna aneko elem-elem keleneko peramag piogti, “Namangkel, mag olog tebatne,” aro molbaog. 18 Molbaogpa kelenekori, “Nani, tululam,” aro mag ma'i aneko elem-elem sa'oag agha toro pibogti mag tero tarogpa teog. 19 Teogpa kelenekori, “Andi pham unta babe mag tebarel phineba mag tukang,” seog. 20 Seogti mag teroba aneko elem-elem pabiogti, pham unta mag teroba anekoag ikatsiogti, nen samoro telbina ikatsiogpa, pham taneko mag ni teek. 21 Undo unulamogpa Abraham arukna anekori, “NANI Allahri nari semban saog uro yepneldo,” aro yubu koma keleneko kembamogti wamog.
22 Pham taneko mag mon og uro teekpa, Abraham arukna anekori wali-walia leng-leng aroba kirik emas agha sunuroba kamna alikinag uro kamna kapto toroba tobogti, kelenekori uramag u pong saog uro tobongkibogti, sae no'obag babe sae supnagha saog uroba phende no'obag tobongkibog. 23 Tobongkibogti hailamogti, “Anina sina etne? Aniri aeag nari maboap mabukapti, olog kolo wamlare?” aro haibaog. 24 Haibaogpa, Ribka nenekori, “Na nani Nahorap Milkaapti mangkaekne Betuel wamla,” seogti, 25 “Nu nununge ae an sembe kolo wamla. Anenge pham mamnebag babe wamlaba, anenge phamdi tena babe wamla,” seog.
26 Wene seogpa Abraham arukna aneko so'oag yabu sanukto pulingkiogti, Allahag yubu omekto towalengelamogti, 27 “Na nani Abraham ILI Allah omeklangken. Andi Nani Abraham ekon tan koma o'ona senelamlamdi, ambarom saog uro ualam. Na nani ilipsaeringe mangaroba wamangag peket enero poa yanelam,” seog.
28 Wene seogpa Ribka neneko ilindi aeag piogti, Abraham arukna anekori lebogne sembe eldi ae lomag nimi ambarelamsiogpa, Ribka eldo Laban nenekori babe ka'ebaog. 29-30 Ka'ebaogti kemelamogpa, ilipsa'el uramag babe, saeag babe kirik emas agha sunuroba leng-leng aroba tobongkiamogpa, Laban kembi agha elem-elem lambaogti, tilamogpa Abraham arukna elenge phamap mag waliroba peramag sekamogpa, Labandi tibog aogti, piog. 31 Piogti, Abraham aruknaag, “Nani, an NUNI Allahri o'ona sembe yeplangkelne wamlamba, ipken. Paiag tane wamlulam kom. Anap anenge phamap mamnebag o'obandi, aeag pukapti yae,” seogti, 32 enero poa aeag piog. Piogti Laban nenekori arukahiogpa pham unta alibag payaogne taneko toro pelengkaekti, pham mamnebag o'ol aba karebaekne usa yangaro uaekti, tena pham sembe babe tatsiek. Tatsiekti Abraham arukna yan mag a'elbaukang aro mag tebatsiek.
33 Kwaneng yoro sin whingag pail aekpa, Abraham arukna anekori lelamogti, “Kwaneng anam ten komag na yanne sembe ambatsineri, na kwaneng tenun,” seogpa, Laban nenekori, “Nani, ambatsi!” seog. 34 Seogpa, “Na Abraham arukna yan,” seogti, 35 “Na nani neneko NUNI Allahri mikip nenelamogti, pham domba, sapi, unta, keledaiap, wali-walia leng-leng aroba kirik emas, perakap, elenge me kelmabo aruknaap maiknoro tarogpa, el neneko tupne taog. 36 Na nani elkel Sara neneko ya'ag sabal taog aghana me nengabo me mangkarogpa, naniri, ‘Na tebane tanena naringe tane name meneri ngai taho,’ senelamlari wamla. 37 Eldi ambarelamneori, ‘Na name sembe Kanaan kelabo ebaheng kom aro sik uro leplulam. 38 Na nani samenag wamoghag pimendi, na nari naipsae anabiagne kelabo nhon ipmendi, enero poa yalulam,’ aro ambatneo. 39 Ambatneoba narina, ‘Kelabo anekori, “Anap Kanaan so'oag pinunam kom,” sele tanena welaro uanun?’ aro haibanoba 40 eldina, ‘Undo kom. Nani Allah yubu ka'elamnaba Nani Allah ponekori, “An yepkilul,” aro Ilipsae malaikat poghol aleba, na name sembe naipsae anabiagne kelabo nhon iplulam. 41 Nangkabo tanekori kelabo nhon tatkik komba, ipmen taneogna, wene salag palilamnange sembe seneragna koma wana aingniro nongog yalulam,’ aro ambatneo. 42 Ambatneoba sumene mag sembe so'o walirobag yingkina wanaag agha molona Allahag yubu lelamnari, ‘NANI, Abraham ILI Allah wamlamne, Abrahamdi sembao saog uro uanundi, yepnululam. 43 Wene wamneba, kelme nhon mag na wamnaag ane tel tol alengeag, “Mag tebatne,” aro molbaneba, 44 kelabo nhon yalengeri, “Nani mag tebatkenun,” sele tanena el neneko Allahri weplange kom sembanun. Kelabo nhon yalengeri, “Nani, mag tebatkeneri, anenge pham unta babe tebarel pihinun,” selenge neneko sembe Nuni Allahri Abraham elme Ishak sembe kel weplange ane sembanun,’ aro Allahag molona molban.
45 Allahag yubu lelamna abeneko Ribka neneko mag ma'i sa'oag sa'oro poplari, mag telengkilba, nari, ‘Mag tebatne,’ aro mag molban. 46 Mag molbanba, mag ma'i elem-elem toro piplari, ‘Nani, mag temenba, anenge pham babe tarel phineba tukang,’ selba ten. Tenba nanenge pham babe mag tarel phil. 47 Tarel phildi yalba nari, ‘An ani etne?’ aro haibanba, ‘Nani Nahorap Milkaapti mangaro arekne Betuel neneko wamla,’ selba, emas agha u pong sunuropne ulomag tobongkipnari, supnagha leng-leng arobari emas agha wiropne saeag tobongkipna. 48 Tobongkipnari, nani Abraham ILI Allah omeklamnari, yabu sanukto pulingkina, ‘Andi na nani ilipsaeri mangarobari mangaroba wamlaag payanelamba, nani elme sembe kel ene imna,’ Allah omekan. 49 Ane sembe a'undi na nani ‘Nu sisa nhonog aneko,’ sembamundi, o'ona sembamun tanena tibin nenero ambatnululom, kom sembamunne babe ambatnumunba, yog kelabo ebanun,” aro ambatsiog.
50 Ambatsiogpa Labaanap Betuelapti ambarelamtekti, ''NUNI Allahri seneropne sunsunum uro, uro yabilba, nuri sa'a senep kom. 51 Ribka nene anap pirumunba, NUNI Allahri sembao saog uro ani elmeri toplul,” serek.
52 Wene serekne ka'ebaogti, Abraham arukna aneko so'oag yabu sanukto pulingkiogti, NUNI Allah omekaog. 53 Omekaogti, ag eneknangeap, leng-leng aroba kirik perak emas agha wiroba saeag tobongkipnepneap, saramag langkanepneap Ribka tarogti, ilin babe eldo babe wali-walia tatsiog. 54 Tatsiogti, Abraham arukna anekoap ilipsaboap kwanengap maghap teekti mabek. Mabeka kwelekaogpa, Abraham arukna anekori, “Na naniag pinundi ambarelamsin,” aro ambatsiog. 55 Ambatsiogpa, Ribka eldoap ilinapti ambarelamdekti, “Ribka nunap ik sa'obare to undo wamebea pilul,” serek.
56 Wene aro ambatekpa Abraham arukna anekori, “NUNI Allahri yeplamneoba, naniri seneragtopne sunsunum uro uano anekona a'undi, ‘Wamukap,’ seheng kom. Naniag pinun anekona, ‘Elemag,’ ambatnururom,” aro ambatsiog.
57 Wene aro ambatsiogpa, sindina, “Ribka neneko yobeberi haibaukap,” serekti, 58 Ribka yoptekti hailamdekti, “Nimi poneap pururom te?” aro haibarekpa, “Nhon pinunam,” seog.
59 Wene seogpa, Ribka ologhag paia ulamog kelenekoap Ribkaap, “Nhon pururom,” aro ambarelamsirekti, “Abraham arukna ilipsaboap nhon pululom,” aro ambatsirekti, 60 Allah siag Ribka kiplamekti, “An nunipsa'el wamlamne nimi teng-tengne a'ilbanep kom saog uro wamukangne sikinin talulam. Andi mangarobari mangkahyikne tanekori nimi isanangdi as soro pin wa'alamikti, oro yaglamsukang,” aro kibek. 61 Kibekpa, Ribkaap eldi arukna tanekoap ag togto aekti, pham unta alibag waelbaekti, Abraham arukna anekoap nhon piek.
62 Abraham arukna palamog ko'o eneko Ishak neneko mag sembe wailbaekag, “Beer-lahai-Roi” engeropne neneko peramag piogti, wamog. Beer-lahai-Roi wamogha Kanaan so'o mag kwasag sip piogti, wamogha samoro yalamog.
63 Ik nhon sum sinag aweag, “Ina kemeroa yabunun,” aro elenge aeag agha lambaogti, tilamogpa pham untari nimi poamsiekpa, yalameka tibogahiog. 64 Ishakti tilamsiogpa, Ribka nenekori babe Ishak tiboghaogti, pham unta alibag agha kuluka yingkiogti, 65 Ribkari Abraham aruknaag hailamogti, “Nimi nhon aweag siba nunag sip yalamlange nene etne?” seogpa, Abraham arukna anekori, “Na nani yalamla ane,” seog.
Wene seogpa Ribka neneko ag agha whing liliptaog.
66 Wene Ishak yaghag yaekti, Abraham arukna anekori undo unuaogne sembe Ishakag ni ambarog. 67 Ambarogpa Ishak nenekori, “Ribka na kel,” aro toro aogti, ilin Sara samen wamog aeag poa wa'iog. Poa wa'iogti, wamekti, Ishak nenekori, “Nanin tebaoba, wana mali uro wamnaag agha na kel Ribka yanel,” aro Ribka sembe o'ona senelamog.
