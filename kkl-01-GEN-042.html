Yusup eldo yabo “Kwaneng roti tol pukap” aro pieka (42:1-24)
42
1 Sikini Yakubti ka'elamogpa, “Kwaneng roti Mesir wamla,” seeka ka'ebaog. “Wamla,” seeka ka'ebaogti, elmaboag yubu lelamogti, “Sa'a sembe namabo aingniro ulamlom?” seog. 2 Nen yubu lelamogti, “Na yubu ka'ebanne ‘Mesir kwaneng roti wamla,’ seanga ka'eban. Kwaneng yo seklero tebabeag, ane sembe pimundi, kwaneng roti kamna toro payalulom,” seog.
3 Wene seogpa, Yusup eldo yabo sa'obare “Kwaneng roti tobukap,” aro Mesir piek. 4 Yakubti elme Benyamin Yusup ilipsae nhon “Peheng kom,” aro salero pibog nena, “Inaag palamikpa, opleag” aro salero pibog.
5 Kanaan kwaneng yo waelbaogpa, nimi orange babe Mesir kwaneng kamna toro tol alamekpa, Yakub elmabo babe “Tobukap,” aro yaek. 6 Kwaneng roti mog so'o ni ane kamna tol alamekpa, tarelamsiognena Yusup. Mog so'o Mesir saelbamogpa, eldo yabo yaekti, sikinipsae Yusup whingag yabu sanukto pulingkina kibek. 7 Yusupti eldo yabo ipsiog aghana, maneri ipsin koma saog uaog. Yusupti, “A'unna tala nimi?” aro yubu yo uro haibahiog. Haibahiogpa, sindi, “Nu Kanaan mog so'oag wamap nimi agha, ‘Kwaneng tobukap,’ aro yaap,” seek.
8 Yusupti eldo yabo whingna iamsiog aghana, eldo yabori elna iamek kom. 9 Iamek komba, Yusup eldi lenda sin sembe malamogne seneraghogti, “A'un nimi tane yalom nena, haing toro nimi wea urop komag mog so'o eneag wamlange kemenelehin nimi yalom,” aro wene seog.
10 Wene seogpa, sindina, “Nuni, undo kom. Nun yaapnena, ‘Kwaneng tobukap,’ aro yaap,” seekti, 11 “Nani, nu yaap nang enena, nun kongap nang. Nai ot nhon. Nu wana porog nimi. Nu kemenelero haing tohirop nimi kom,” seek.
12 Wene seek, aghana eldi lelamogti, “Orolena. A'un yalomne mog so'o ene ‘Yae ua unne sogna wamlaag kembaukap,’ aro yalom,” seogpa, 13 Sindina, “Nu kongap nang aobare nang. Nu Kanaan so'oag wamap, nuri nai ot nhon. Nhonna loba taog pana, wenena elabome nen tagha iliap wamdang,” seek.
14 Wene seekpa, Yusupti, “Yo! Usaap taneko lemna, a'unna haing toro kemenelehin nang,” seogti, 15 “Wene selom aghana, a'un nari mipsinun. Nari sik uro nimi nubunge yubu sia leropne siag lelamnange, anam a'unipsae anam yan komag a'un nhon babe pululom kom. 16 A'un agha nhon wepmunba, pileri, a'unipsae tol pilul. Tol piamleba, nennena sae kaupto piamsinun. A'undi leplomne sik talul to? A'undi leplomne neneko orolena tanena, sik uro nimi nubunge Firaundi lepso saog uro nari lelamnangena a'unna kemenelehin nang,” seog.
17 Wene seogti, ni kop aeag tobongkipsiogpa, ik wilindi wamek. 18 Ik wilindip sum Yusupti sinag yubu lelamogti, “Na Allah sembe log uro wamnange. Nari a'un sae lipsinunne, ot ina nhon. 19 A'undi leplomne sik sembanepne sembe, a'un agha nhon kop aeag salero pibukap. Orangena kwaneng tobomne popmundi, a'undi kelabo me kwaneng yo seklelamangne sembe pabululom. 20 Pumun aghana, a'undi leplomne sik senena sembe a'unipsae elabome neneko payalulom. Kom tanena tam ikinne nari a'unag pimnun,” seog.
Wene seogpa, “Yo,” seek.
21 Sin mamun yubu lelamekti, “Wene nunag tam talamlangena, samenag nuri nunipsaeag uabunge sembe, ‘Naro yabo omneheng kom,’ aro wene alamog aghana, nuri el sembe o'ona sembabu komdi, obubu. Aneko sembe wene tangoro nunag talamla tam nene seklelamap ane,” alamek.
22 Wene alamekpa, Rubendi ilipsabo tanekoag yubu lelamogti, “ ‘Obukap,’ alamlumba, nari ‘Opseng kom moaba apmunagti,’ aro ambatsisi kom te. Wene alamsi aghana, ka'ebanulum kom. Wene el tebaogne neneko eneng aneko sembe nun eneng lunup talul,” aro wene alamek. 23 Wene alameka Yusuptina ka'eamsiog aghana, eldo yabori seneragamek kom. Sindi lelamekne nhon meri Yusubag yubu tiplamog.
24 Wene alamekpa, Yusup sin laploa piogti, engel piogti, yaogti, “Simeon haing kauplulom,” aro ambatsiogpa, elkabo whingag agha salero haing kaupto pibek.
Yusup eldo yabo samoro Kanaan pieka (42: 25-38)
25 Simeon sae salero pibekpa, Yusupti eldi aruknaag ambarelamsiogti, “Sindi ag poa yaongneag kwaneng lilapmundi, kamna tobong kirik solomne samoro sindi aghag libahilulom, apna kwaneng inaag tenepneap,” seog. 26 Libaekpa, Yusup eldo yabo taneko kwaneng taneko popsirop pham keledai alibag towalengkibekti, palamek.
27 Mamnebag aneko piekti, sin taneko nhondi, “Pham keledai kwaneng tatnun,” aro ag ina siram kwhabia agha kemelamogpa, kirik solomne kwaneng kamna tobog kirik eneko ag ina siramag wamog. 28 Wamogpa, ibogti, elkabo nenneag ambarelamsiogti, “Nangkabo, nari aghag kembamun! Kirik solomne naringe samoro pibang,” seogpa, kembaekti, sin mamun kemeptaom agha log lelem pet-pet alamekti, “Nuni Allahri nunag sa'agha ulamla?” alamek.
29 Kanaan sikini wamoghag piekti, sinag uro yabiogne sikiniag ambarekti, 30 “Mesir so'o saelbamlange ponekori hagto walamsildi, ‘A'unna mog so'o eneag wamlange haing toro kemenelen nimi,’ alamsio. 31 Aghana nuri elag lelamobori, ‘Nuna wana porog nimi, nuna haing toro mog so'o kemenelen nimi kom. 32 Nuna kongap nang aobare nang naina ot nhon. Nhonna ka'aseptaog bana, wenena nunipsae elabomeog iliap Kanaan wamdang,’ sebo. 33 Wene seboba, mog so'o Mesir saelbamlange ponekori nunagna, ‘Na a'undi leplomne sembe mipsinun. “A'un sik wana nhonog nimiro,” aro a'un sa'obare wamlom nang ene agha nhon nanag wamleba, kwaneng kelabo me si tarapnange sembe kwaneng pabululom. 34 Pabumundi, a'unipsae elabome neneko nanag payamun taneogna, nari a'un sembe, “Haing toro mipna nang kom, wana nhonog nang,” sembahineri, a'unkae lambatsine taneogna, mog so'o eneag hae pang yang ulamlulom,’ aro wene seo,” aro sikiniag nebarek. 35 Nebarekti, pabieka taneko toro la'anilamekti, kemelamekpa, kirik solomne kwaneng sembe kamna kapto tobeka taneko ni nangdinge aghag ubu piamogha sikiniap kembaekti, log senelamek.
36 Kembaekti sikiniri lelamogti, “A'undi unulamlomdi, namabo ka'asebel talamang. Yusup orog taog, Simeon orog tao, wene Benyamin pabunualom. Nanagha sol olamnelomba, ulamnun,” aro wene alamog.
37 Sikiniri wene seogpa, Rubendi iliag yubu lelamogti, “Nani, wene selam aghana, Benyamin na saeag tatnululam. Samoro payane kom tanena, nari mabo me phende ya'ag opsululam,” seog.
38 Wene seogpa, iliri, “Name, elna anap nhon pilul kom. Eldona tebaogba, ilipsae ot nhonog wamla. Palamtumunba, inaag agha ople tanena, penelamnelomne sembe seneragto ulamneri, kul hong yabukom agha teplomnun,” seog.
