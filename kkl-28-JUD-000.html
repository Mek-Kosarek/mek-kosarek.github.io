Yudasti Mome Toro Pibog Yubu
El nenerop yubu
Yudasti mome nene tobogti, “Yakobus ilipsae wamna,” seog aghana, Yudas nene etne, Yakobus etne sembe olog ekon wamap. Nimi si Yudasap Yakobusap nimi maikno wamek ane sembe, nimi nhondi, “Yudas neneko Yesusap, Yesus ilipsae Yakobusap, sikinipsae taptopne wamogti, mome tobog,” senerop (Matius 13:55ap, Markus 6:3ap kembanep). Aghana nimi nhondi, “Yesusti wepto pogsiog nang agha nhon sina Yudas wamogti, mome tobog,” senerop (Lukas 6:16ap, Pogsiog Nang 1:13ap kembanep). Yudas neneko sembe olog ekon wamap aghana, mome nene tobogne ponekona, Yesus aisanangag pan khebaogne Yudas Iskariotti mome tobog kom. Mome nene tobogne ponekori Yesusag “Sikne” aro seneraglamogti, Yudasti elkabo Yesusag seneragtop nimiag, “Orolenange ambatsirop nimiri yubu sik sembamunagti, wana whi uro wapmundi, yae up tangkamlulom,” aro mome nene toro pipsiog.
