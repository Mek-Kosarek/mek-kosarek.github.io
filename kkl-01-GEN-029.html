Yakub neneko elawang Labanag piogha (29:1-14a)
29
1 Undo wene seogti, Yakub neneko Kanaan so'o laplobi agha heng walelingkirobag sip piog. 2-3 Palamogti, tilamogpa, mag sembe wailbaeka nhon peramag pham domba palu wilindi longoro wamekpa, tibogahiog. Ora sum babe pham paia uhirop nang taneko niri sikindinge pham mag aneko peram ni yalamekpa, kembiag agha nubu kirik mag ina siram leptop kirik kupto pail pindogha pham taneko mag tebarelamsiekpa, ni telamek tanena sereklamek. 4 Yakub mag aneko peramag piogti, pham taneko paia uhirop nang tanekoag hailamsiogti, “Nangkabo, a'un tala nimi?” seogpa, sindina, “Nu Kharan nimi,” seek. 5 Seekpa Yakub nenekori, “Nahor ilisag Laban neneko olog ililamlom, te?” seogpa, sindi, “Ililamap,” seek.
6 Wene seekpa, Yakubti, “El welaro wamla?” seogpa, sindi, “Wali wamla,” seekti, “Elmangkel si Rahel pham domba kambing iliringe poa yalamsil ane tiboghamen,” seek. 7 Seekpa, Yakub nenekori, “Nangkabo, ae imbal komba ae siro wamap, aghana sa'a sembe a'undinge pham poloro pail ahilom? Mag aghabog tebatsimundi, ‘Mo salik malik maneng telamukang,’ aro poa phululom,” aro ambatsiog. 8 Ambatsiogpa, sindina, “Pham domba paia urop nimi taneko nenneri pham domba ni payahiikpa, mag ina siram kirik kubeberi, ‘Mag tebatsukap,’ aro tibogto wamap,” seek. 9 Yakub neneko sinap yubu lero wamekpa, Rahel neneko pham iliringe poa yalamsiog. 10 “Na nanin eldori kelmeri pham domba iliringe poa yalamla,” aro tibog aogti, Yakub neneko mag waliroba peramag piogti, kirik mag inasiram leptopne neneko toro ko'ol piogti, Labandi pham taneko mag tebatsiog. 11 Tebatsiogti, Rahel somoro kibogti, “Nari nawang elmangkelap haiptanam,” aro engelamog. 12 Engelamogti, “Na an ani ilipsa'el Ribka elme yan,” aro ambarog. Ambarogpa, Rahel neneko iliag merero piogti, “Anipsa'el Ribka elme yal,” aro ambarel piog. 13 Ambarogpa ka'ebaogti, Laban neneko elem-elem Yakubag piogti, somoro kibogti, eldi aeag enero poa yaog. Aeag yaekti, Yakub nenekori elag uro yabiogne sembe Laban ambarogpa, 14 Laban nenekori, “Otsik, nari enengdinge agha yalam,” seog.
Yakub nenekori Leaap Rahelap topsiogha (29:14b-30)
Yakub neneko wal nhon ningki wamogpa, 15 Laban nenekori Yakubag yubu lelamogti, “An na enengdinge wamlam ana, sa'a sembe nari awe mo webarelamnululam? Kamna etpare tatkenun sembamlam?” aro ambarog.
16 Laban elmangkelabo samenne Lea wamogpa, amikne Rahel wamog. 17 Lea haing pabo tolaktop saogpa, Rahel saren kemeroba wamogti, haing elela aneko wamog. 18 Undo wamdekpa, Yakubti Rahel sembe wana telamogti, “Nawang, an anmangkel amikne neneko toro anundi, nari andi awe welamne paghabog hengkun taibuka taubuka ulamleba hengkun saekpare talul,” aro ambarog.
19 Wene aro ambarogpa, Laban nenekori, “Name ane nimi orangeri toro aikagti an tatkinundi, an nanap tane wamnunam,” seog.
20 Wene seogpa, Yakub nenekori, “Rahel toro anun” aro awe welamogha hengkun saekpare taog. Yakubti “Rahel tomnun,” aro wana telamogpa, hengkun saekpare wamog aneko aghana, “Ik a'ilbanep saog uro wamna,” senelamog.
21 Wene senelamogha Yakub nenekori Laban ambarelamogti, “Nuri lemnomo saog uro hengkun saekpare awe webanori, anmangkel toro anundi, tatnululam,” aro molbaog. 22 Molbaogpa, Laban nenekori, “Namangkel Yakubti toplulne sembe sani yobanun,” aro nimi ni as eneko wamekne yopsiogpa, sani yobaogne teek. 23 Teekti, imbaogpa, Laban nenekori elmangkel toro anepne Rahel payan koma, Lea enero Yakub tarel piogpa, nikag maptek. 24 Undo unuaog sumeneko Laban nenekori eldi arukna Zilpaag, “Lea arukna wamlulam,” aro ambarog.
25 Lukun Yakub pukogti, kemelamogpa, “Leaap mamnam,” aro el taog. El taogti, Labanag piogti, ambarelamogti, “Sa'a sembe nanag nene saogne ualam? Andi, ‘Rahel tatkenun,’ seomba, anenge awe welamno aghana, sa'a sembe yubu neplamnelam?” aro ambarog.
26 Ambarogpa, Laban nenekori ambarelamogti, “As eneag wamlange agha uan. Eldo'el wamnaba, ilipsa'el samenag nengabori tomnep kom,” seogti, 27 “Ane sembe Lea tobomne sembe awi yeng uro seplamebe paghabog, ik saekpare taleba, namangkel Rahel babe tatkenun. Tatkeneba, el sembe kamna nen hengkun saekpare awe naringe welamlulam,” aro ambarog. 28 Ambarogne neneko sembe Yakubti, “Hae,” sembaogti, inipna kwelekna saekpare seplamek paghabog, wamekpa, Labandi elmangkel Rahel tarog.
29 Undo unuaog sumeneko Laban nenekori el arukna si Bilha nenekoag, “Rahel arukna wamlulam,” aro ambarog. 30 Yakub neneko Rahelap babe nikag maptek. Yakubti Rahel sembe agha wanaag uro wamogti, Lea sembe olog wana abolag wamog.
Sin wamekti, Yakubti Labandi awe nen hengkun saekpare welamog.
Yakubti me liklamsiogha (29:31-35)
31 Yakub neneko Lea wanaag olog abolag saogti, NUNI Allah kembaogti, “Lea nenekori me mangaro ale,” sembaogti, “Rahel me mangaro an koma wamle,” sembaog.
32 Wene sembaogpa, Lea me monag taogpa, me nengabo me nhon mangaro aog. Mangaro aogti yubu lelamogti, “Na wana mali uro wamnaba, NANI Allahri imnelba, na na'angeri na babe wanaag sembanele,” aro me aneko si Rubena engkaog.
33 Wameka nen Lea neneko me monag taogpa, me nengabo me nhon mangaro aog. Mangaro aogti, yubu lelamogti, “Na na'angeri o'ona sembamnel komne sembe NANI Allahri ka'ebaneori, me ane babe tatnel,” aro me aneko si Simeonb engkaog.
34 Wamekti Lea nenekori me nengabo me nhon mangaro aogti, yubu lelamogti, “Me nengabo me wilindi mangkatnonge sembe na'angeri na sembe wanaag uro wamlul,” aro wene seogti, me si Lewic engkaog.
35 Wameka Lea neneko me monag taogpa, me nengabo me mangaro aogti, yubu lelamogti, “Wenenge nene agha Allahag ‘NANI’ aro kipto omeklamna,” aro me aneko si Yehudad engkaog. Yehuda mangaro yabiogne agha Lea me mangaro an koma wamog.
