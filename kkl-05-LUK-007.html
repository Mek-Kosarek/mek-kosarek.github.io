Yin salerop nang sikindo arukna Yesusti wali nembaogha
(Matius 8:5-13; Yohanes 4:43-54)
7
1 Yesus nimi maikno taneko whingag wene aro yubu ambatsiogti, Yesus as Kapernaum wa'iog. 2 As Kapernaum pioghag aneko yin salerop nang sikindo Romange eldi arukna o'ona senelamogne naikna naiklamog. Naikna naiklamogti, loba tanep saog wamog. 3 Yin salerop nang sikindo ponekori Yesus sembe yubu ka'ebaogti, nubunang Yahudi nimi sembe wenehirop nang etpare poglamsiogti, “Yesusag pimundi, elag molbamunba, yaleri nari arukna wali nembalul,” aro pogsiog. 4-5 Sin pogsiog nimi taneko Yesusag piekti, wanaag agha sarikto-sarikto ambarelamekti, “Nai, yin salerop nang sikindo ponekori nu Yahudi sisa nimi sembe o'ona sembamsildi, nuri asag Allah yubu lero winirop ae sobatsiog. Ane sembe eldi, ‘Yepnoho,’ aro molona molbaghelba, yeplulamne sunsunum senelamap,” aro ambarek.
6 Wene aro ambarekpa, Yesusap nhon piek. Palamekti Yesus ae enekoag wa'al pineba peramag palamekpa, yin salerop nang sikindo ponekori elkabo Yesus yalamogha whingag pogsiog. Pogsiogpa piekti ambarelamekti, “Nungkae ponekori anag ambarelangkel yubuna, ‘Nai, na sisa Yahudi komne wamna. Nari aeag an mandu aro opkeleagti, an nimi nubunge wa'al ananne olog sunsunum komdi na nimi ologneri aeag yanep kom. 7-8 Wene aro seneragnari, na babe an nimi nubunge whingag yanepne sunsunum kom. Ane sembe andi yubuag agha nari arukna, “Wali talul,” semenba wali talul. Nimi mikipneri yubu leplenge sik uro yabinepne sembe na el wamna. Na babe aruksirop yubu umag wamnari, ka'ero saelba ulamna. Nari yubu lelamnange babe na umag nimiri ka'elamangdi, saelba ulamang. Nari nimi nhonag, “Pululam,” senep tanena, nimi eneko pinep. Nari nimi orangeag babe lelamnari, “Yalulam,” senep tanena yanep. Nari arukna nhonag, “Undo ualulam,” senep tanena nari, “Ualulam,” senne sunsunum uro eldi uanep. Ane sembe an nimi nubunge wamlamdi, mo yubu lepmenba, wali talul,’ seldi yubu pipla,” aro Yesusag ambarek.
9 Sindi ambarek yubu Yesusti ka'ebaogti, lo'om seog. Lo'om seogti tangabom agha nimi maikno elag neleplamek nimiag yubu lelamogti, “Yubu lemnunne ene ka'eamsut. Nimi eneri nanag sik tapto, ‘Mikipne,’ aro wana paliro seneraglamnel. Eldi nanag sikne aro seneraglamnel saog uro Israel nimiri nanag undo seneragtop nimi nhon babe ia ulamsin kom,” aro wene seog.
10 Yin saleropne ponekori pogsiog nimi taneko samoro aeag piekpa, eldi arukna aneko wali taogpa ibek.
Yesusti as Nain yauog keldi me kamag nembaogha
11 Ik ko'o maikno wamna komag anekoa Yesus as Nain piog. Yesus eldi yubu ka'ero neleptop nimiap elag nimi maikno mo kemero neleplamek nimiap nhon piek. 12 Yesus palamogha leka as kanero solingkirop ina siramag anam sekel wa'in komag, nimiri nimi teptoba keyapto asag agha poa lambaek. Tebaogne poneko ilinna yauog kel wamog aghana, eldi me nhonogne eneko tebaog. Tebaogpa as eneko nimi maiknori wana o'ona paibatto ilinap nhon poa palamek. 13 Nu Saelba Uhiropne Yesusti kel eneko ibogti, wanaag agha yareag sembaogti kel enekoag, “Nanin, engkaheng kom,” seog.
14 Wene seogti nimi teptoba morea kalag keyapto poa yalameka anekoag Yesusti sae pibog. Sae pibogpa, nimi keyapto poa yalamek nang taneko pabekpa, Yesusti teptop kabuni enekoag yubu lelamogti, “Meo, nari anag lelamna. ‘Puke,’ sen,” seog.
15 Wene seogpa, tebaog kabuni eneko pukogti, yubu lelamog. Pukogti yubu lelamogpa, kabuni eneko Yesusti sae kiliro ilin saeag samoro tarog. 16 Nimi ni wamek nimi tanekori ibekti, log senelamek. Log uahiogpa, sindi Allah omeklamek. Yubu lelamek nena, “Allah yubu silimu tiptopne nubunge nu anabiag wene lag tal! Nuni Allah wene yingkina, ‘Nari mabo yepsinun,’ aro kemelamsil,” aro Allah omeklamek. 17 Sindi Yesus sembe lelamek yubu neneko sabo tangto piogpa, mog so'o Yudea wamek nimiap, ni kanero urop mog so'oag wamek nimiap tanekori ka'elamek.
Yohanes maghag pelengehiropne ponekori, “Yesusag hail pululom,” aro nimi pogsiogha
(Matius 11:2-19)
18 Yohanesti yubu ka'ero neleptop nangdi Yesusti nia ulamogne sembe Yohanesag ambarek. Ambarekpa Yohanesti sin nimi phende yopsiogti, 19 nu Saelba Uhiropne Yesusag poglamsiogti, “A'un Yesusag pirumundi, haibarurom nena, ‘Anna Allahri samenag, “Yalul,” aro salag pibogne poneko agha yaom to, amik nimi nhon yogne yalulne tibogto wamukap to?’ aro hail pururom,” aro pogsiog. 20 Sin pogsiog nang abeneko Yesusag pirekti hailamdekti, “Yohanes nimi maghag pelengehiropne ponekori anag, ‘Hail pururom,’ aro pogsilba anag yanam. ‘Hail pururom,’ sel nena, anna Allahri samenag, ‘Amik pognenge nhon yalul,’ aro salag lero pibogne poneko an sembe lebog to, nimi nhon amik yalulne tibogto wamukap to?” aro haibarek.
21 Yohanesti pogsiog nang abeneko Yesusag yarekti, haibarek sum enena, Yesusti nimi naikna nhon saog nhon saog nimi wali nenelamsiogti, phia yonge nimi kulomag ua ulamsiogne yag lanalamsiogti, haing urop nimi maikno haing kweleklamsiog sum eneko yarekti haibarek.
22 Wene aro haibarekne sembe Yesusti sinag samoro, “A'un samoro Yohanesag pirumundi, a'undi haingdi iptomne babe, aori ka'ebaromne babe ambatturom. Ambarel pururom nena, ‘Nimi haing uropne kwelekto ililamang. Yan mali nimi yan ingiro palamang. Nimi kon tena pololamsil nimi kut talamang. Ao lepsirop nimi ao kuplamsil. Teptop nimi kamag talamang. Apna Allah Yubu Walinge nimi saeagha orog nimiag ambatto palamla,’ aro ambarel pururom. 23 Nimi etneri na Yesusti ulamnange sembe wa'ebanele kom tanena, nimi eneko wana awi uro wali wamlul,” aro ambatsiog.
24 Yohanesti pogsiog nimi sin phende abeneko samoro pirekpa, Yesusti alniro nimi maikno longolamek nimi tanekoag Yohanes sembe ambarelamsiogti, “A'undi, ‘Sa'a ibukap,’ aro pai aldesag aneko pilum? ‘Phoanari pongoroba ko nhon pho alamoba, tara sip pang, tara sip yang ulamonge ibukap,’ aro pilum te? 25 Undo kom aghana, a'un pilumne, ‘Sa'a ibukap,’ aro pilum? ‘Ag walinge agha enekto amog nimi ibukap,’ aro pilum te? Kemerop ag walinge wangal torop nimi sindi kwema orogti wanaag, ‘Topsene,’ senenne torop nimi tupnang agha wamang. Sin ot yubu sia leropne ae nubungeag ua urop. 26 Ane sembe a'un pilumne, ‘Sa'a ibukap,’ aro pilum? ‘Allah yubu silimu tiptopne ibukap,’ aro pilum te? Undop pagha tanena, otsik nari babe a'unag lelamnange, Allah yubu silimu tiptopne wamla. Ot Yohanes ponekona, Allah yubu silimu tiptop nang ni anabiag agha el mo paliag uropne wamla.
27 Yohanes sembe samenag nimi nhondi mome toro pibog nena,
‘Nari yubu poa ambatsirop nimi nhon an whingag pogneba,
an amik pinep ina an sembe yarelamlul,’
aro mome toro pairopne wamla.
28 Sik uro ambarelamsin. Mog so'o eneag mangap taek nimi ni anabiag agha Yohanes ponekori yubu ambatto tongolamlange nubu sirikti, el saogne nhon wamla kom. Aghana Allahri saelba ulamsil nimina, ora nimiri sin nimi taneko sembe, ‘Nimi ene olog sirikne,’ sembahinep. Aghana Yohanes olog el umag wamlaba, Allahri Saelba Ulamsil nimi taneko paliag nenelamsilba wamang,” aro Yesusti ambatsiog.
29 Wene aro Yesusti Yohanes sembe ambatsiog yubu ene nimi ni wamek nimi tanekori ka'ebaek. Ni ka'ebaek nimi tanekona, Yohanesti samenag, “A'undi malia seneragto ulamlomne lipsimundi, Allahri senenne salelapmunba, maghag pelengkipsinun,” aro ambatsiogne sunsunum uro uaek. Sin Roma nimi sembe kamna poloro torop nangdi babe Yohanesti ambatsiog yubu sunsunum uro ulamek ane sembe, Allah yubu ka'elamek nimi ni tanekori, “Sikne aghabog wamla,” aro lag phoro lero ulamek. 30 Aghana sin Farisi nimiap, Musa yubu amohirop nimiapti, “Nuri malia seneragto ulamapne libukap kom,” senenne agha Yohanesti yubu saelban koma Allahri, “Ulamukang” senenne wa'eplamekpa, Yohanesti sin samenag maghag pelengkipsiog kom.
31 Nen Yesusti yubu lelamogti, “Nimi wene ko'o wamang nimi tane wela saog nimi wamang? Nari sunumna huropneag agha sumbahinun? 32 Sinna nimiri kamna toro longorobag olog mabo puka urop mabo saog wamang. Wharo pulamangdi, yobol dalamangdi,
‘Nu ma'i lom toman pho aro sunukna sunuklamap aghana,
a'un sepna seplom kom.
Nu engenange lelamap aghana,
engena engkalom kom,’
aro wal dalamlonga saog wamang.
33 Wene alamnange tam nene sembe. Yohanes maghag pelengelamsionge poneko yaogti, kwaneng ten koma ulamogti, anggur wana lolterop mag yonge tero koma wamogpa, a'un wene ko'o wamlom nimi tanekori, ‘Phia yonge el kulomag wa'ioba ulamla,’ aro waro lelamom. 34 Na Yesus mog so'oag nimi tahingena, kwaneng mag tero yalamsi aghana, a'undi yubu lelamlomdi, ‘Kembahut! Elna kwaneng kolag linne wamlari, mag yongeri wana toropne wamla. Elna Roma nimi sembe kamna poloro torop nangap, malia urop nimiap sikinkae wamla,’ aro waro lelamlom. 35 Nu sembe waro lelamlom aghana, wana heksiropne saelba urop nimi niri ul palamangne nimiri kembi agha el tanep nena, ‘Wana hektopneri senenne sikne aghabog wamla,’ aro nimiri el tanep,” aro Yesusti ambatsiog.
Malia urop keldi Yesus kibogha
36 Farisinge nhon sina Simon ponekori Yesusag, “Kwaneng nari aeag tununam,” aro yobog. Wene aro yobogpa, Yesus Simondi aeag wa'ina, “Kwaneng tununam,” aro pul piog. 37 As enekoag kelabo nhon, nimiri el sembe, “Mali-malia aghabog urop kel,” senerop kel nhon wamog. Kel enekori ka'elamogpa, “Yesus poneko Farisinge Simondi aeag kwaneng telamla,” aro wene alameka ka'ebaog. Ka'ebaogti kel enekori nongag pelagtop mag song walinge payaog. Nongag pelagtop mag song walinge nene kirik hogtoba lom nhonag ikto tobongkibekpa, eldi saeag taogpa payaog. 38 Payingkina Yesus wailag sip yanag sekel ingkina engelamogti, hail mag agha Yesusti yanag alniro pelaglamogti engelamog. Yesusti yanag hail mag agha pelaghongkia kel enekori usog hongdi Yesus yan lupto meklamog. Yan lupto meklamogti, kel enekori Yesus yanag togtog aro kiplamogti, mag song walinge neneko agha liklamog. 39 Simon Farisinge Yesusag, “Kwaneng nari aeag tununam,” aro yobogne ponekori kemelamog. Kemelamogpa kel enekori Yesusag unuaogne sembe Simondi wanaag seneraglamogti, “Nimi pone Allah yubu silimu tiptopne wamso tanena, el yan salelamla kel ene sembe el wamnep nena, ‘Malinge maikno urop kel,’ aro el tanep,” aro wanaag yubu lelamog.
40 Wene aro wanaag yubu lelamogpa, Yesusti Farisinge ponekoag, “Simon, nari yubu nhon ambatkenun,” seogpa Simon ponekori, “Nai, yubu amolamsilamne! Andi ambatnemenne ka'ebanun,” seog.
41 Wene seogpa Yesusti sunumna nhonag tol pina ambarelamogti, “Nimi phenderi tup nimi nhonag pina a'ero-a'ero pirekti mololamdek. Nhonna, ‘Kamna kaptoba kirik wana solomne teng supna tarabare tatnemenba, ni lumbanun,’ aro molbaogpa nhonna, ‘Teng phende agha tatnemenba, ni lumbanun,’ aro molbaog. Tup nimi enekori sindi molbareka sunsunum uro tatsiog. 42 Tatsiog aghana, sin ni phende abenekori sindi salag lero pairopne sunsunum uro kamna kaptoba kirik wana solomne nene lumbanep kom. Lumbanep kom ane sembe, sin phende abenekori lemnepti, ‘Nu saeag orogti andinge kareploroba lumbaghanep kom,’ serekpa tupne ponekori ambarelamsiogti, ‘Naringe a'undi sae kareploroba nen samoro lumbanululom kom. Nari tam wailag nembarelamsin,’ seog. Wene seogpa, sin phende abenekori tupne poneko sembe, ‘Naba Naniong,’ aro o'ona sembanep aghana, nimi nelari wanaag agha o'ona maikno sembalul?” aro Yesusti hairo ambarog.
43 Wene aro haibaogpa, Farisinge Simon ponekori, “Nari senerop nena, kamna nubunge saeagha lumbanep komne ponekori tupne poneko sembe, ‘Nari kamna tam kom nembal,’ aro o'ona maikno sembalul,” seog.
Wene seogpa Yesusti, “Andi leplamne otsik uro tam karebalamdi leplam,” seog.
44 Wene seogti, Yesusti kel enekoag kembi agha Simonag yubu lelamogti, “Andi kel ene iplam te? Na andi aeag yan aghana, andi nari yan mag a'elenne wero paiamlam kom. Ot kel eneri nari yan eldi hail magti nari yanag pelagto a'elbaldi, eldi usog hongdi lupto mekla. 45 Na usa yanba, andi somoro togtog aro kipnelam kom aghana, kel eneri papna koma nari yan togtog aro kiplamnel paghabog tal. 46 Andi nari usoghag kal zaitun wana lolterop mag agha, ‘Kipnun,’ aro ikto pelaghanelam kom aghana, kel eneri kamna alikinag urop mag song walinge nari yanag ikto pelaghanel. 47 Ane sembe sik uro ambarelangken. Kel eneri malia ulamonge maikno sirik aghana, el kel eneri nanag o'ona senero ulamlange sembe eldi malia maikno uroba tam wailag tal. Aghana yog nimiri malia ologha uropne tam wailag nemban tanena, nimi enekori tam wailag nembanne ponekoag olog uro o'ona senerop,” seog.
48 Wene seogti, Yesusti kel enekoag yubu lelamogti, “Andi mali-malia ulamlamne tam wailag tal,” seog.
49 Wene seogpa, Yesusap kwaneng nhon nikag telamek nimi tanekori sin mamun alniro lel dalamekti, “Allah Elmori nimiri malia uroba wailag nembanep. Nimi ene, ‘Etne wamna,’ sembaldi kel enekoag, ‘Andi malia uropne tam ni wailag nemban,’ aro lepla,” aro wanaag seneraglamek.
50 Aghana Yesusti kel enekoag yubu lelamogti, “Andi nanag sikne aro wana palilamlamne nenekori an wali nembaghel. Ane sembe an wana aingniro pululam,” seog.
