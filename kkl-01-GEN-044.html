Yusupdinge mag tena walinge ka'eseptaogha (44:1-17)
44
1 Yusuptinge ae yae ua uropne ponekoag ambarelamogti, “Kwaneng sindi poa pineba, uro lilamsimendi, sindi kirik solomne kamna tobika aneko nen samoro sindi ag ina siramag libahilulam. 2 Naringe mag tena solomne agha sunuroba anena sikinipsae elabongeri ag ina siramag libalulam, apna kirik solomne kwaneng toplange kamna anekoap nen samoro eldi ag ina siramag libalulam,” seog.
Wene seogha sunsunum uro Yusupti arukna aneko uaog.
3 Kwelekaogpa, lukunap ae tibin talamog abeneko Yusup arukna tanekori “Pululom,” aro sae lipsiekpa, poahirop pham keledai taneko alibag towalengkibekti, palamek. 4 Mesira lambaekti, anam we uro pin komag Mesir so'oag palamekpa, Yusupti eldi ae yae ua uropne ponekoag yubu lelamogti, “Elemag sarekerepto sin palamikag pululam. Sarekerepto sinag pimen tanena, sinag leplulamnena, ‘Sa'a sembe nairi walia ulamlaba, a'undi naiag yoaba ulamlom? 5 Sa'a sembe nairinge mag tena solomne agha sunuroba haogha tobom? Mag tena anena, nai mag tero el amik unag sembe pom-pomoro el tan ana. A'undi yoaba nubunge uaom,’ sululam,” seog.
6 Wene seogpa, Yusupti ae yae ua uropne poneko Yusup eldo yabo inaag palamekag piogti, Yusupti ambarogne sunsunum uro lebog.
7 Wene aro ambatsiogpa, Yusup eldo yabo tanekori, “Sa'a sembe nuniri undo lepla? Sa'a sembe elag nuri yoaba uahabe? 8 Samen sum eneko babe kirik solomne nuri ag ina siramag wamonge babe samoro payabonge an el wamlam. Welaro nuri nairinge aeag ua uroba kamna maiknoro toroba kirik emas to perak to welaro topsebe?” seekti, 9 “Nuringe aghag etneri aghag agha mag tena ane ipmen tanena, wamleba, ipmen me eneko tebalul apna nai arukna taukap,” aro ambarek.
10 Wene aro ambarekpa, Yusup arukna anekori, “Wali leplom aghana, etneri aghag wamle tanena, el neneko agha nai arukna talul. Wene nennena hae pululom,” seog.
11 Wene seogpa, sindinge ag pham keledai alibag agha pelengero so'oag pibekti, ag si kwha-kwha yabiek. 12 Ag si kwhabaekpa, Yusupti arukna ae yae ua ulamogne ponekori sen-sen agha yandongeri aghag samenag alnibaogti, eroba elabongeri aghag. Elabonge Benyamindi aghag elamogpa, mag tena Yusupdinge neneko Benyamindi aghag wamog. 13 Eldo yabo taneko sikinipsae sembe o'ona ua ahiogpa, sindinge ag enekto amekne saeri taepto unuaekti, pobeka taneko pham keledai alibag towalengkibekti, samoro Mesir piek.
14 Yehuda ilipsaboap Yusupti aeag palamekpa, Yusup aeag wamogpa, pina elemag yabu sanukto pulingkina kibek. 15 Kibekpa, Yusupti lelamogti, “Sa'a agha a'undi ualom? A'unna ekon te? Na saogne pone a'undi ulamlomne ag pom-pomoro yoaba ulamlomne ipsenep,” seog.
16 Wene seogpa Yehudari, “Nai, nuri anag yubu lemnep kom. Sa'a agha lebukap. Welaro agha nuri, ‘Malia uan kom,’ senep kom. Allahri nu anmabori ulamapne ane sel. Wene nu ni ane an arukna taukap. Mag tena eldi aghag wamlange neneko aghabog kom,” seog.
17 Wene seogpa, Yusupti, “Kom! A'un nenne a'uniag samoro wali uro pumunba, elba naringe eldi aghag wamlange nenekoog, nari arukna talul,” seog.
Benyamin sembe tiro Yehuda yubu lebogha sembe (44:18-34)
18 Wene seogpa, Yehuda sekom agha Yusup peramag piogti, “Nani, na nen olog yubu lemnun. Na wabaneheng kom, an nimi nubunge yubu sia leropne saog uro Mesir wamlam. 19 Samen yabonge neneko nai andi hailamsiomdi, ‘A'undi nai babe a'unipsabo babe wamang te?’ seom.
20 Wene seomba, nurina, ‘Nuri nai sabal tao, elme olog me nhon wamsilnena, sabal taoag agha toro aonge. Sin phendena sikinin ot nhon. Eldona tebaog aghana, eldi maum tattopne agha wamsil, ane sembe iliri mene sembe wanaag uro ua ulamla,’ sebo. 21 Nai, andi ‘Payamunba, kembanun,’ seoma sembe payabo. 22 Aghana nuri nai anag ambarelangkebori, ‘Menena iliri sae limnep kom. Mene agha ili sae limna tanena, waba kaong senep saog,’ seboba, 23 andi lelamomdi, ‘A'unipsae elabonge neneko agha payamun kom tanena, na whingag yalulom kom,’ seom.
24 Wene seomba, Kanaan mog soo ag pibori, nuri nai Yakub an yeplangkelne ponekoag ambarelamobori, andi lebomne abeneko ambarobo. 25 Ambaroboba, nairi ‘Nen kwaneng tol pululom,’ aro arukahio. 26 Aghana nuri naiag lelamobori, ‘Nun mona pukap kom. Nunipsae nhon taneogna pukap. Nunipsaeap pibe kom tanena, nimi enekori whing ibukap kom,’ sebo.
27 Wene aro nai ambaroboba, nairina nunagna, ‘Namabo, a'un el wamlom. Nari kel Raheldi me phendeog yag nembitsio. 28 Me samenagne ka'aseptaog, yoabari teog to. Ka'aseptaog aneko olog haiptangtop kom. 29 Wene babe name elabonge nene na saeag agha topmunba, uro yabinep komne uro yabile tanena, na sabalag anda nanagha tipto loba nembanumunba, tebanun,’ seo,” aro ambarog.
30-31 Nen tipto ambarelamogti, “Nani, ane sembe nanagha naipsae koma naiag pine tanena, andi arukna nuri nai loba talul. Eldi hainna meneag tiptangkamla, apna el ya'ag sabal taobana, nuri sap wana mali nembabe tanena, sap tipto loba nembaukap. 32 Apna nari naiag salag lelamnori, ‘Na saeag piplamba, nari me ane o'obanun,’ seno. Nari agha, ‘Me ane samoro payane kom tanena, tam ualea nanagha sa'obanun,’ seno. 33 Ane sembe nanagha mene lunumna an arukna taneri, tane wamnun. Me anena sae lipmenba, eldo yaboap nhon pukang. 34 Mene koma nanagha naiag panne sunsunum kom. Na mene koma na naiag panba, naiag uro yabinep komne uro yabanne nenena, na haingdi ilina mali,” seog.
