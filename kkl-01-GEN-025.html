Abrahamdi yog kelaboag agha nimi liksiogne (25:1-6)
25
1 Abrahamdi yog kelabo Ketura nhon tobog. 2 Ketura tobogne nenekoag agha me mangkahiognena: Simiranap, Yogsanap, Medanap, Midyanap, Isibaghap apna Suaap mangkahiog.
 
3 Abraham elme Yogsandi mangkahiognena Siabaapna Dedanap.
Dedan sisana Asyur nimiapna Letus nimiap Leum nimiap.
4 Abraham elme Midiandi mabona Epha, Epher, Henokh, Abida, apna Elda'aap mangkahiog. Mabo enekona ni Ketura elauyabo wamek.
5-6 Abrahamdi yog kelabo topsiogti, me pipsiogne sae agha olog-olog tatsiog. Me pilopsiogne tanekoag, “Heng walelingkirobag sip pimundi wamlulom,” aro yag lambahiogti, el sae agha nia mangkina Ishak saeag aghabog pibog.
Abraham tebaogpa so'o sikeka (25:7-11)
7-8 Abraham mangkaeka hengkun taibuka taubuka ulamogha teng no'opbare eptopne naualbareag (175) wamogpa, sabal taogti, wana aingniro wamogti, hain kom taogpa, ilisag yaboag yame samen piog. 9 Tebaogpa, elmabo Ishakap Ismaelapti Makhpela kirik arameag so'o siktekag aneko Mamre peramag so'o samen Hetne Sohar elme ngai wamogpa, 10 Abraham nenekori kamna kapto tobogti, elkel Sara kirik arame so'o sikoghag aneko Abraham babe so'o siktek. 11 So'o siktekti, wamdekpa, Allahri Abraham elme Ishak neneko o'ona senelamogti, yeplamog. Li ko'o eneag Ishak neneko mag sembe so'o wailbaekag, “Beer-lahai-roi” so'o peramag wamog.
Abraham elme Ismaeldi nimi liksiogne (25:12-18)
12 Abraham elme Ismaeldi elmabo liksiogne ane. Abraham elkel Sara arukna Hagar Mesir kel wamogti, Abrahamap maptekti, Ismael mangkaog. 13-15 Ismael elmabo likto piogha sunsunum uro sin sina ane:
Ismael elme yandome Nebayor,
nabime Kedar,
tanena Adbeel, Mibsam, Misima, Duma, Masa, Hadad, Tema, Yetur, Nafis, apna elabome Kedmaap.
16 Ismaeldi mabo sin aobare nangene sindi sisa aobare taogpa, sin nimi nubunang as a'ero sin si nubu taog. Ismael elmabo as babe kalun ae longoro sorobag babe a'ero-a'ero solamekpa, ua ulamekag a'ero-a'ero elmabo si sunsunum uro alamek.
17 Ismael el mangkaekne hengkun-kunne teng lambare eptopne phende (137) wamogti, hain kom taogti, ilisagyaboag yame samen piog. 18 Tebaogpa, Ismaeldi mangaroba sin mog so'o wamekne Hawila ina agha Syur ina agha heng walelingkirobag sip Mesira Asyur simbik wamek. Sin wamekne sikin kaboap aisa nenep tangto wamek.
Esauap Yakubap mangkahieka (25:19-26)
19 Abraham elme Ishak nenekori ulamogne ane:
20 Ishak mangkaekne hengkun taibuka taubuka ulamogha teng nhon eptopne naualbare (40) taogpa, elkel Ribka toro aog. Ribka eldo Labanap sikini Betuel Aramneap Aram mog so'o nimi wamdek.
21 Ribka neneko me mangaro an koma wamogpa, Ishak nenekori Allahag molona yubu lelamogpa, NUNI Allahri ka'ebaogpa, Ribka me monag taog. 22 Me monag phende wamdekti, webeltalamdekpa, Ribka nenekori, “Sa'a sembe tamna nene nanag ulamla?” aro wanaag agha seneraghogti, “NANI Allahri tibin nenero ambatnilul,” aro hailbiog. 23 Hailpiogpa, NUNI Allahri yubu lelamogti, “Nimi sisa phende an monag wamdang. Sin-sin aisa neneptangkamtukang. Sin phende abeneko nhon agha mikipne wamleba, nabinge paliag taleba, yandonge el arukna talul,” aro ambarog. 24 Ambarogpa, wamogti, Ribka nenekori me phende mangkahiog. 25 Samenag mangap taogne neneko kon uwong hong mana hong saog uro pongoropne mangap taog. Undo pongkamogha sembe me si Esaua aro engkaog. 26 Me amikne neneko mangaro kuluklamogti, eldori yan pubu saelbamogti, eldi, “Na sam,” aro yan saelbamogne sembe si Yakubb engkaek. Ishak hengkun teng phende eptopne no'opbareap (60) wamogpa, sin phende mangkahiog.
Eldo Esau Ilipsae taogpa Ilipsae Yakub eldo taogha (25:27-34)
27 Me abeneko nubu tarekti, Esau neneko pham winang mana sae yonge wamogti, paiag pham winang mana onne yeng awi uro wamog. Yakub neneko wana aingniropne wamogti, aeag aghabog ua ulamog. 28 Ishakti winang mana sop tenne sembe awi yeng uro wamogti, elme Esau sembe agha wanaag uro ua ulamogpa, Ribka nenekori elme Yakub sembe wanaag uro ua ulamog.
29 Ik nhon sum Yakub nenekori kacang uwongne yolamogpa, Esau neneko paiag agha kwaneng yo uro yaog. 30 Yaogti Yakubag, “Nangkae, na kwaneng yori uwonga ane olog tatne!” aro molbaog. “Uwonga ane,” seogne sembe si nhon Edomc aro engkaek. 31 Esau nenekori molbaogpa, Yakub nenekori ambarelamogti, “Wali molbanelam, ane sembe anna yandonge wamlama anekona, nimi yandoa na tatnemendi, anna nabinge tamenba, na yandonge tanun taneogna, molbanelama tatkinun,” seog. 32 Molbaogpa, Esauri, “Kwaneng yori tebanep saog ulamnari, sa'a sembe ‘Na nimi yandoa wamna,’ senun?” seog. 33 Seogpa, Yakub nenekori, “ ‘An nimi yandonge an wamlulam,’ aro Allah siag sik uro leplulam,” aro ambarogpa, “Otsik uro lemna,” seogpa, Yakub neneko nimi yandoa aneko taog. 34 Undo unuaogpa, Yakub nenekori kwaneng rotiap kacang uwonga anekoap tarogpa, teogti, piog.
Undo ulamdekti, “Na yandonge ua una karong,” aro lipsiog.
