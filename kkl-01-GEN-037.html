Yusupti lenda mabogti, eldo yabo nebatsiogha (37:1-11)
37
1 Esau ilipsae Yakub neneko ili nimi sekelanan nang saog uro wamoghag Kanaan so'oag wamog. 2 El elmabori ulamekne ane:
Yakub elme Yusup mangkaekne hengkun taibuka taubuka ulamogha timbom agha ko'olom tarabare taogpa, iliri elkelabo nembahiogne taneko Bilhaap Silpaap sikin mabo mangkahirekne tanekoapti pham kambing domba piamsiek. Yusupap nikag piamsiekti, eldo yabori yoaba ulamekne sembe Yusup nenekori ili ambarelamog.
3 Yakub nenekori elme Yusup sembe, “Sabalag agha mangaro ano me,” aro wanaag uro seneraglamogti, eldo yabo sembe wana abolag uro seneraglamsiog. Ik nhon sum Yakubti ag nhon ketkero yogto-yogto wibogti, “Enekel dolamlul,” aro tarog. 4 Yakub elmabo yogneri seneraglamekti, “Nuniri nunipsae Yusup sembe wanaag wamlari, nun wailag nenelamsil,” aro el taekti, sikinipsae Yusubag yubu wali uro lelamek kom.
5-7 Ik nhon sum Yusupti lenda mabogti, eldo yabo nebatsiog. Yusupti eldo yabo nebatsiogne ane, “Naro yabo, lemnunne nene ka'eamsut! Nari lenda malamnaba, nun ni aweag wamapti, roti wana mauroba yo'oa kareptopne kaupto palilamapti, kemelamnaag agha nari kaupto pililamnange sek-sek yabiamangba, a'undinge yomoro naringeag kiniplamang,” aro ambatsiogpa, sindi el sembe, “Karong,” senelamek.
8 Wene aro ambatsiogpa, elag yubu lelamekti, “ ‘Na yubu lenne taneri, nu mombolag nembahinun,’ senelamlam?” aro wabaek. Lenda mabiagha nebarelamsiogne sembe eldo yabo taneko yo senelamek.
9 Undo unulamekpa, nen lenda nhon mabogti, eldo yabo nebarelamsiogti, “Nari lenda mamnaba, hengap, walap, imberea ko'olombareap tanekori na saramag yabu sanukto kulukul alamangba, imna,” aro ambatsiog.
10 Lenda mabogne neneko ili babe ambarogpa, iliri walamogti, “Lenda nene wela saogne maplam? Na aniap, aninap, ando yaboap, ‘Nanag yaikti, yabu sanukto pul aukang,’ senelamlam?” seog.
11 Undo unulamogne sembe Yusup eldo yabo tanekori el sembe wana mali lelem karong uro seneraglamekpa, iliri lenda aneko el uro ua ulamogti, usoghag paia ulamog.
Yusup kamna kapto tobeka (37:12-36)
12 Wamekti, Yusup eldo yabori pham kambing domba sikiniringe taneko poa pihiekti, as Sikhem peramag piamsiek. 13 Undo unuaekpa, Yakub nenekori Yusup ambarelamogti, “Ando yabori pham nuringe as Sikhem paiamangdi, wamangag kemel philulam,” aro ambarogpa, Yusupti, “Nani, wali leplam,” seog.
14 Wene seogpa, Yakubti, “ ‘Ando yaboap, pham naringeap, wali wamangdo,’ aro kemel phimendi, ambarel anululam,” aro ambarogpa, Yusup neneko Hebron whaboghag agha “Sikhem pinun,” aro palamog. “Sikhem pinun,” aro palamoghag agha 15 “Tala wamang,” aro ekondi, paiag tangom ina ag tangom aba palamogpa, nimi nhon haiptarekti, “Sa'a elamlam?” aro haibaog.
16 Wene aro haibaogpa, Yusupti, “Nani, ‘Naro yabori pham tala piamsiangdi, tala wamang,’ aro elamsindi, an el wamlam tanena ambatnululam,” seog.
17 Wene seogpa, nimi enekori, “Ando yabo tane lambaongdi, palamongdi, ‘As Dotan pukap,’ aro wene alamonga ka'ebahino,” aro ambarogpa, Yusup neneko eldo yabo as Dotan wamekag piogti, tibog ahiog.
18 Yusup neneko weag yalamogha tibog aekti, “El nunag yalamle tanena obukap,” sembaekti, 19 sin mo-mo yubu lelamekti, “Lenda polan uro maropne poneko yalamlaba, 20 ya'ag oro mag longkanep kirik arameag pelengkaukap. Undo unuaberi, ‘Si yoabari obo,’ alamukap. Undo obebeba, eldi lenda malamonge welaro pilul kembaukap,” alamek.
21 Undo wene alameka Ruben nenekori ka'ebahiogti, “Welaro sin saeag agha taulbanun?” sembaogti, yubu lelamogti, “Naipsabo, ya'ag obukap kom. 22 El eneng yingkileag, nuri elag sa'a-sa'a uan koma mag longkanep kirik arameag ani pelengkaukap,” seog. Wene seogne nene, “Yusup neneko obikag pelebaneri, ‘Aniag pululam,’ senun,” aro seneraghogti, lebog.
23 Yusup neneko eldo yaboag yaogpa, sindi saelbaekti, elenge ag walinge poro amogne neneko kuripto tobogahiekti, 24 sae kiliro pabiekti, mag longkanep kirik arameag aneko peleng lobol piek. Peleng lobol piekag aneko mag longorop komag peleng lobol piek.
25 Unuaekti kwaneng telamekti, tilamekpa, yog nimi Ismael sisa nimi mog so'o Gilead anekoa “Mesir pukap,” aro yalamek. Yalameka anekona, kal wea nimi whesinaap, teptop nimi song kom nenenna-ap, kwanengap ma'aro yonaap, “Nimi Mesir wamang nimiri kamna kapto tobukang,” aro pham unta alibag towalengkibekti, payalamek. Sindi payalameka tibogahiek. 26 Tibogahiekti, Yusup eldo Yehuda nenekori elkaboag yubu lelamogti, “Nangkabo, sa'a sembe nunipsae obeberi, eneng tognobaukap? 27 Yusup nunipsae ana, obukap kom semban. Ane sembe Ismael nang eneag kamna kapto tobukap,” aro ambatsiogpa, “Wali leplam,” seekti, 28 wamekag Ismael nimi yalamekpa, ipsiekti, “Midian nimi yalamangne ane,” sembaek. Midian nimi wali-walia kamna kapto tororo yabirop nimi yabaekpa, Yusup neneko eldo yabori mag longkanep kirik arame anekoa kiliro towalengkaekti, Ismael nimi taneko tatsiekpa, sindi kamna kapto kirik wana solomne lin tarabareag tobek. Tobekpa, Yusup neneko Mesir poa piek.
29 Poa piekpa, Yusup eldo Ruben mag longkanep kirik arame anekoag yaogti, tiplengelamogpa, Yusup neneko kom wamogpa, lo'om seogti, ag wangaro amogne kweldero epsiog.
30 Undo unuaogti, elkaboag piogti, ambarelamsiogti, “Me aneko orogti, wene wela uanun?” seog.
31 Seogpa, Yusup eldo yabo tanekori pham kambing nhon obekti, Yusupti ag eneko pham kambing enengag werebaekti, 32 nimi nhon ambarelamekti, “Ag ane nuri naiag pabimendi, ambarelapmendi, ‘ “Ag ane nuri ibobori, anmeri ag to?” aro pebel alangken,’ sululam,” aro ambarekpa, Yakub yaghag piog.
33 Piogti, ag aneko pebogpa, Yakub nenekori kembaogti, yubu lelamogti, “Ot sik uro nari nameri ag. Name Yusup neneko si yoabari oro kaelbaori, pelekto teo,” seogti, 34 wana maliri ag wangaro amogne kweldero epsiogti, kuripto tobogahiogti, “Amang ulamna,” aro ag kilikin ag malinge poro aogti, so'o liktangto elme sembe sana amang wamog. 35 Amang wamogti, wamogpa, elmabo elmangkelabo yingkina “Wana aingnibaukap,” aro yaek aghana, sikini wana aingnilamog kom. Elme sembe seneraglamogti, “Name sembe amang ulamneri tebanun,” seogti, engero aghabog ua ulamog.
36 Undo wamogti, ua ulamogpa, Midian nimiri Yusup neneko Mesir pabiekti, Potifar ponekoag tarel piekpa, eldi kamna tatsiog. Potifar neneko Mesirnang sikini yae wamek nang sikindo wamog.
