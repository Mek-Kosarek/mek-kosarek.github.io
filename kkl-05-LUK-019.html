Zakheus kal araag waelbaogti, Yesus tibaogha
19
1 Yesus, “As Yerusalem tipto pinun,” aro as Yerikho aneko wa'ina palamog. 2 As Yerikho enekoag nimi nhon saeagha tupne nhon sina Zakheus wamog. Zakheus poneko Roma nimi sembe kamna poloro torop nimi sikini nhon wamog. 3 Zakheus ponekori, “Yesus yalamlange poneko wela saog nimi wamlange imnun,” aro ulamog aghana, nimi maikno sendebel alamekpa, el nong purundi Yesus imnep kom wamog. 4 Ane sembe Zakheus el samenag merero menangal piogti, “Yesus yalamlenge imnun,” aro kal ara nhonag waelbaogti, tibogto wamog.
5 Kal araag waelbaogti wamogpa, Yesus kal ara tamag aneko sekel piogti tiwalengkaogti, “Zakheus elemag kulukulae! Na sumene, ‘Tala mapsene?’ sembamnari andi aeag pinemeri wamnunam semban,” seog.
6 Wene seogpa, Zakheus poneko elemag kulukul aogti, yeng uro kipto tolamogti, Yesus enero poa eldi aeag piog. 7 Eldi aeag enero poa wa'alamogpa, nimi wamek nimi tanekori, “El Yesusna sa'a sembe malia aghabog ulamla nimi enekori aeag wa'al pil?” aro parog-parog alamek.
8 Wene alamek aghana, Zakheus sekogti, Yesusag yubu lelamogti, “Nai, nu Saelbamsilamne ka'ebalulam! Nari tuba saeag wamlange nari pogto phende nembaneri, nhon nandamne sembe pimneri, ‘Phendep nena nimi saeagha orog nimi sembe tatsinun,’ aro lag phoro lelamna. Apna na samenag, ‘Roma nimi sembe kamna kaptoba poloro payalulom,’ aro aruklamsino aghana, nari phelektopne salero teno tanena, phelektopne nene nen lumbanun. Nimiag samoro tatsinun nena, sindi saeagha phelekto tomnonge aghabog tatsinun kom aghana, nen ma'aro ik tombare lumbaloa saog uropne tatsinun,” seog.
9-10 Wene seogpa, Yesusti nimi tanekoag, “Nunisag Abraham samenag wamogti ulamog saog uro ae ene ngainge poneko babe nelepto uropne wamla. Na mog so'oag nimi tangtopne yahingena, ‘Nimi ka'eseptangtop nimi ebahineri, wali nenero taulbahinun,’ aro yahi. Nunisag Abrahamdi samenag ulamog saog uro, wamap ae ene ngaingeri babe elag nelepto ual ane sembe, sumene Allahri nimi taluro saelbahinepne ae lom aneag keknep tal,” seog.
Tupne nhondi eldi arukna saeag pipsiog nimiag sunumna tol pina Yesusti ambatsiogha
(Matius 25:14-30)
11 Yesusti yubu ambarelamsiogne as Yerikho wamek nimiri ka'elamek. Ka'elamekti sindi senelamek nena, “Yesus wene as Yerusalem palamlari, nuri as Yerusalem peram wamabag yal. Undop pagha, Yesusna Allahri wepto poghogne poneko wamsori, Yerusalem pile sum eneko el mori alniro nu sembe yubu sia lero saelbamsiso,” sembaek. Undo sembaek ane sembe, Yesusti sunumna yubu tol pina nimi tanekoag ambarelamsiogti, 12 “Nimi nubunge nhon wamogti, ‘Weag mog so'o nhonag pineba, na yubu sia leropne towalengkaneik tanena, samoro yanun,’ sembaog. 13 Wene sembaogti, anam pin komag eldi arukna sa'obare yopsiog. Yopsiogti sinag kamna kaptoba alikinag uropne leng-leng aroba emas agha sunuropne wanange sin a'ero-a'ero nhon-nhon tatsiog.a Tarelamsiogti yubu ambatsiog nena, ‘Na pineba, wamne komag a'undi kamna kaptoba wanange tatsinne neneag agha na sembe nen ma'aro nubu nembarelamnumuna kekman samoro yanun,’ seogti sindi saeag nembaog.
14 Wene aro tatsiogti, piog aghana, as eneko wamek nimiri el sembe sin karongdi nimi poglamsiekti, ‘A'un pimundi, nimi eneko towalengkipnep nimiag ambarelapmundi, “Nimi neneko sembe nu saekag komdi, el yubu sia leropne towalengkipseng kom,” sululom,’ aro nimi pogsiek. 15 Pogsiek aghana, tup nimi nubunge poneko towalengero, ‘Mog so'oag wamang nimi sembe yubu sia lelamlul,’ aro sekirekpa, nen samoro eldi as enekoag yaog. Yaogti, ‘Nari kamna kaptoba tatsinonge sindi nubu nembaong to?’ aro eldi arukna samen kamna kaptoba wanange tatsiog nang eneko yopsiog.
16 Wene aro yopsiogpa, nimi samenag yaog nimi enekori ili nimi nubunge ponekoag ambarelamogti, ‘Nai, andi kamna kaptoba wanange ot nhon tatneomne nenekoag agha nari o'olamnori, nen wana mauamloa saog uropne sa'obare nembano,’ seog.
17 Wene eldi arukna anekori, ‘Sa'obare nembano,’ seogpa ili ponekori, ‘An sik uro nari arukna walinge wamlam! Nari ologha andi saeag pimno aghana, andi wali sik uro saelbaom. Ane sembe nari an towalengkagheneba, as soro kalurop as sa'obare nimi sembe yubu sia lelapmendi, na umag saelbamsululam,’ seog.
18 Eldi arukna phendepne eneko ambarel aogti, ‘Nai, andi kamna kaptoba wanange ot nhon tatneomne nenekoag agha nari o'olamnori, nen wana mauamloa saog uropne lambare nembano,’ seog.
19 Wene seogpa, ili ponekori eldi arukna anekoag, ‘Nari an towalengkagheneba, as soro kalurop as lambare nimi sembe yubu sia lelapmendi, na umag saelbamsululam,’ seog.
20-21 Eldi arukna wilindipne neneko yaogti ambarelamogti, ‘Nai, nari an sembe el wamnari, “Nari nai nimi yonge logne wamla,” sembano. Andi nimi luknuhiro nimiri saeagha uropneap, andamneri wero meroan komneap sop-sop taluro tolamsilam. Ane sembe nai, andi samenag kamna kaptoba wanange tatneomne neneko, “Ka'eseptaleag,” aro ta'asoma lomag wali uro wa'amogto paiamno. Kembalulam. Wene ane wamlaba, nen samoro tarelangken,’ seog.
22 Wene seogpa, ili ponekori samoro yubu lelamogti, ‘An nari arukna wana yonge wamlam. Andi na sembe senelamnelamdi, “An nimi yonge wamlamdi, nimi luknuhiro nimiri saeagha uropneap, andamneri wero meroan komneap sop-sop taluro tolamlam,” aro yubu ambatnelam te? Andi wene aro yubu ambatnelam yubuag agha nari an sembe tam lelekto karebaneri, anag ikinne pelengkanun. 23 Andi na sembe, “Elna nimi undopne wamlaba na log,” sembahom tanena, sa'a sembe kamna kaptoba nari tatkenonge neneko poa nubu nembanep nimi saeag andi pail pihinep kom? An sindi saeag pail pindi, “Kamna kaptoba neneko ma'aro nubu nembaleba, nani nen samoro yaleba, ma'aropne nene naniag talul,” aro uahom. Undo unuahom tanena, nari tatkenongeap, nen phelekto ma'aropneap topsene,’ seog.
24 Wene seogti, nimi nubunge ponekori nimi wamek nimi tanekoag ambarelamsiogti, ‘A'undi kamna kaptoba wanange nhon ane eldi saeag agha toplulom. Topmundi elkae sa'obare nembao nimi enekoag tatlulom,’ seog.
25 Wene seogpa, nimi wamek nimi tanekori lelamekti, ‘Nai! Elna kamna kaptoba wanange sa'obare salelamlaba, “Nen tatlulom,” selam te?’ aro wene seek.
26 Wene seekpa, nimi nubunge yubu sia leropne ponekori sinag samoro yubu lelamogti, ‘Nari lelamnange ka'ebahut. Nimi etneri elag wamlenge, “O'oro saelbanun,” senelamle tanena, elag tatnepne nen ma'aro yabilul. Aghana nimi etneri elag wamlenge, “Limnun,” senelamle tanena, elag wamlenge babe kom talul,’ seog.
27 Wene seogti, nimi nubunge ponekori nimi tanekoag ambarelamsiogti, ‘Wene pimundi, nimi nanag aisa nenelamneangdi, “Nu as enekoag nimi sembe yubu sia leropne talul kom,” alamong nimi tol pihina payahimundi, na whingag anea ya'ag opsululom,’ seog,” aro Yesusti sunumna yubu ambatsiog.
Yesus nimiri kiplamekne toman agha as Yerusalem wa'iogha
(Matius 21:1-11; Markus 11:1-11; Yohanes 12:12-19)
28 Yesusti ni aneko wene aro ambatsiogti, sin whing toman agha menangaro palamogti, Yerusalem sip walelamog. 29 Yerusalem sip walelamogti, yim Zaitun tamag sorop as Betfageap as Betaniaap aneko peramag piog. As phende abeneko peramag wamogti, eldi yubu ka'ero neleptop nang phende el whingag samenag pogsiog. 30 Poglamsiogti, “As nu whingag wamla asag anet pururom. Pirumundi wa'alamtumun abeneko tilamtumunba, pham keledai me nhon kerekamika ipturom. Iptumun pham keledai ene alibag nimi nhon babe pham ene alibag pulingkirop kom pham kerektoba iptumundi, pham keledai ene lopto poa yarurom. 31 Loplamtumunba nimiri a'unag, ‘Sa'a sembe pham ane loplamdom?’ aro hailogsiik tanena, a'undi samoro ambatsururom nena, ‘Nu Saelba Uhiropne ponekob teko ualba pabalamnam,’ sururom,” aro ambatsiog.
32 Wene aro ambatsiogpa, sin phende Yesusti pogsiog nang abeneko samenag pirekti kemelamdekpa, Yesusti ambatsiogha sunsunum uro wamogha iptek. 33 Iptekti pham keledai aneko loplamdekpa, pham ngainang tanekori hailamsiekti, “Sa'a sembe pham keledai ane loplamdom?” aro haibahiek.
34 Wene aro haibahiekpa, sindi samoro yubu ambarelamdekti, “Nuni nu Saelbamsilne ponekori, ‘Teko uanel,’ selba loplamnam,” serek.
35 Wene serekti, pham keledai me aneko lopto poa leptekti, Yesusag poa yarek. Poa yarekti, sindi ag nongag enektop ag kwiripto Yesus puknebag pham keledai alibag aneko yangki agha Yesus keyapto pham keledai alibag pukirek. 36 Pham keledai anekoag pulingkiogti palamogpa, nimiri sindi nongag enektop ag kwiripto, “Kibukap,” aro Yesus yalamog ina toman yangalamek.
37 Yesus palamogha Zaitun yimag agha kulukul ingkirobag anekoa as Yerusalem tiboghanebag palamogpa, eldi yubu ka'ero neleplamek nimi niri alniro Allah omeklamek. Sindi samenag agha Yesusag neleplamekpa, Yesusti ik sulu Allah mikibari uanepne ulamogha ililamekne sembe awiri yubu nubu uro Allah omeklamek. 38 Sindi yubu nubu uro lelamekti,
“Nu Saelba Uhiropneri poglaba,
el siag kekhaekto yubu sia lenne yalamlange poneko paliag uropne omeklamsut!
Imagne ponekoap aisa nenep tan koma ma'ap tangkamukap.
El paliag sirik imag wamlange omeklamsut!”
aro omeklamek.
39 Wene alamekpa, Farisi nimi agha nimi etpare sin anabiag wamekne tanekori ka'ebaekti, Yesusag lelamekti, “Nai, andi nimi amolamsilamne wamlam. Andi yubu ka'ero neleptop nimiri an sembe yubu lelamangne sunsunum komdi sinag, ‘Yubu kom tahut,’ aro wembahilulam,” aro ambarek.
40 Wene seekpa, Yesusti samoro sinag, “Nari a'unag sik uro lelamna. Sindi babe yubu kom taheng tanena, sindi wene lelamang saog uro kirik so'oag ubu paiamangne taneri babe haum alamseng,” aro sinag samoro ambatsiog.
41 Yesus as Yerusalem peramag piogti, as soro kalurop as Yerusalem aneko kembi agha Yesus hail mag pelengero engkatsiog. 42 Engkarelamsiogti, “A'un as Yerusalem nimi sumene wana hekahisoba, ‘Undo unuaberi, nu aisa nenep tan koma aingniro wamukap,’ aro el tahom tanena, a'un wali wamnep. Aghana a'un whingag wamlange ililamlom komdi, a'undi wana seneragnep kom. 43-44 A'un as Yerusalem nimi taneri na Allahri wepto pogneogne yalamnange iamnelom kom. Ane sembe, a'unag amik uro yabilul nena, a'un aisa nimiri as Yerusalem ane tembom-kaembom aro sekel yaikti, a'un kanero, ‘Nimi opsukap,’ aro wea uroba sobaukang. A'un aisa nimiri wea wa'aikti, a'un as Yerusalem nimi olopsukang. As ene tembom-kaembom agha ni kwerekto pin serepna nembahiikpa, kirik pairo poa walerop kirik enekoag agha nhon babe nen sorobag sekamlul kom,” aro Yesusti as Yerusalem nimi engkatsiog.
Yesusti Allahri Mem Ae aramag wa'ina nimi yag lambahiogha
(Matius 21:12-17; Markus 11:15-19; Yohanes 2:13-22)
45 Yesus Allahri Mem Ae aramag wa'iogti kemelamsiogpa, nimi maikno wa'ina pail alamek nena, “Yingkirop nimiri Allah sembe tatnepne kamna kapto tobukang,” aro pail alamekpa, kamna kapto tolamek. Yesusti ipsiogti, kamna kapto payalamek nimi yag lambahiog. 46 Yag lanalamsiogti yubu ambarelamsiogti, “Samenag Allahri yubu lebogne nimiri mome toro pibog nena,
‘Nari ae enena, na omektop nimiri molona molorop ae wamlul,’ seog.
Aghana a'undi ulamlomneag agha, ‘Nimi ohiro haoghaba torop nimi tognop tangtobag’ saog nembaom,” aro Yesusti yag lambahiog.
47 Yesus sop-sop Allahri Mem Ae aramag wa'alamogti, nimi yubu ambarelamsiog. Yubu ambarelamsiog aghana, memnang Allah sembe palirop nang sikini yaboap, Musari yubu amohirop nangap, Yahudi nimi nubunangap tanekori, “Yesus obukap,” aro pa'asea sum elamek. 48 “Obukap,” aro ulamek aghana, Yesusti yubu ambarelamsiogne nimi maikno ka'elamek nimi ni tanekori sindi wanaag, “Yubu ambarelamsilne wali uro ka'elamsebe,” senelamek ane sembe, Yahudi nimi nubunangdi, “Yesus saelbaberi obukap,” sembamekne ina eraruk wamog.
