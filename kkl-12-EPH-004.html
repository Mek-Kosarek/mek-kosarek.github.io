“Wana seneragna nhonog uro wamukap,” aro ambatsiogha
4
1 Nangkabo! Nanap nu Saelba Uhiropne ponekoap ma'al dalamnamne sembe haing sae kaupto kop aeag tobongkimneong. Ni yubu ambatsinne nene kilbi agha, nari a'un wenelamsin nena, Allahri a'un yopsiori, “Nari mabori undo unulamukang,” senenne sunsunum uro ul palamlulom. 2 A'undi ni ul palapmunne, sop-sop wana mombolag nenel dalapmundi, nimiag win-win aropne ulamsululom. Nimiri a'unag malinge ulamikpa, wana pail angkalapmundi, malinge paip tan koma o'ona senel dalamlulom. 3 Allah Yame Walingeri nu wana aingniro ma'alamsildi, nu wana nhon seneragtop nimi nenelamsil. Allah Yameri nu ma'alamsil ane sembe a'undi, “A'ep tabeag,” aro wali uro yae up tangkamlulom. 4 Yame nhonog wamlari, nu ma'asinne agha nong nhonog nembahiog. Eldi a'un yopsiogne sunsunum uro, “Nu amik wali wamukap,” aro tiro nang-nangaro seneraglamapne babe nhonog wamla. 5 Nu Saelba Uhiropne ot nhonog wamla. Nuri elag sikne aro wana palilamapne, seneragna nhonog. Nu Kristusap ma'ap tangkamapne sembe maghag pelengehinne saelba ulamapne anena nikne, nhonog wamla. 6 Nuni Allah ot nhonog wamlari, nu nhin saelbamsildi, ni pere agha awe tolamlari, niag ua ulamla.
7 Nu a'ero-a'ero wamap nimi ni sembe Kristusti, “Tatsinun,” senelamlange sunsunum uro nunag wana yorobahinneag agha tarelamsil. 8 Tarelamsilne nene sembe, anamag mome toro pairop yubu nhon wamlange ane.
“El alikin sirikag walelamogti, elap mal tarop nimi agha
nimi sae haing kaupsirop nimi menangaro poa waelbahiogti,
mog so'oag nimiag wali-walia tarelamsiog,”
aro mome toro pibog.
9 “El waelbaog,” aro mome toro pibog tanena, anam waelban komag samen el mog so'o eneag kulukul aogti wamog. 10 El kulukul aogne poneko, el babe imag paliag alikin sirikag waelbaog. Undo waelbaogne nenekona, “Ni to'opto liro pimnun,” aro imag paliag waelbaog.
11 El mori, “Nimiag tatsinun,” senelamlari tarelamsilneag agha, nenne eldi wepto pogsirop nimi nenelamsildi, nenne Allah yubu silimu tiptop nimi nenelamsil. Nenne Allah yubu walinge pabiro ambatsirop nimi nenelamsildi, nenne eldi elmabo paia uhirop nimi nenelamsildi, nenne nimi amohirop nimi nenelamsil. 12 Eldi nimiag, “Awe tolamlulam,” aro undo tarelamsil andarina, nu elmori wepsirop nimiri wero yebel dalameberi, nu Kristus nongag tangtop nimi taneko noro mikip taukap. 13 Eldi nunag tatsiropne sunsunum uro awe tolamebe paghabog, nu niri Allah Elme ponekoag wana paliro elap nimi pali wamapne pere agha, wana seneragna nhonog uro taukap. Nu Kristusti nongag wamap nimiag ni to'opto palilamsilne sunsunum uro, “Wana whi uro wamsebe,” aro wana seneragna nhonog uro wamukap.
14 Wana whi uro wamebe tanena, olog mabo limituk uro orolenange sik senelamang saog uro yog nimiri yubu mo ka'ero saelbamukap kom. Orolenange nimiri ambarelamsiang yubu sik senero neleptopne anena, khao pho alamlari, tara sip yomol pang, tara sip yomol pang ulamla saog uro orolena yubu nelepto ka'elamang. Undo nelepto ka'elamang nena, mag kula'eptobari nimi kiliro opsiloa saog uro orolena yubu neneri ina selepne toman poa palamsiang. Aghana nu wana whi uro wamebe tanena, nimiri sindam nimi wana seneragtopneag agha, “Haing kumbahineri, yubu ambatsinenge nelepto ka'ebanukang,” aro ambatsiik yubu neneko saelban koma nen nelepto sik sembamukap kom. 15 Orolenange nelepto saelbamukap kom aghana, ot o'ona senehinneag agha yubu sikne mo salero lelamukap. Yubu sikne mo salero lelameberi, nia mangkina ul palamebengeag agha nuri usog Kristus ponekoap ma'ap tangto wana khaptangkamukap. 16 Elap ma'ap tangto el nongag tangtop nimi wamap. Nimi nongag yo'o kopkobap, yo'o luluagneap ni wali uro tiptangkamlari, usogap nhon tiptangkamla taneogna, nimi nong mikip talamlari nolamla. Nangkabo, a'un Kristus nongag tangtop nimi wamlom nena, eldi awe tarelamsilne sunsunum uro yepto o'ona senel dalamebe tanena, el nong nolamloa saog uro elag agha nubu talamukap.
“Samen ul piropne lilobi agha wendogne ul palamlulom,” aro ambatsiogha
17 El nongag tangtop nimi wamap ane sembe, nu Saelba Ulamsilne ponekoap ma'ap tangto wamapneag agha nari ambatnuahinne nene sikne aro ambatnuahin. A'undi samen Allah ekon nimiri loba seneragtopneag agha ul palamangne saog uro ulamlum aghana, nen undo unulamlulom kom. 18 Sindi seneragna inibin wamsilba, wanaag ka'ebanep kom saog ane sembe, sin ekondi, Allahri kamag nenehinne sinag tahinepne neneko a'el dalamang. 19 Wanaag ali sembanep komdi, sindi wanaag bobo walelamsilba, “Agto yaboho,” senenne og neleplamangdi, aboapti kelabo nengabo salik-malik teklol dalamang.
20 Aghana a'unna, Kristus el tangtop nimiri undo unuanep kom. 21 A'un sik uro el sembe ka'ebaomdi saelbaom te? Yesusti yubu ka'ebaomdi, elap ma'ap taomneag agha a'un amoro Yesusti yubu sikne sunsunum uro ambatsiong te?
22 Sik uro a'un amoro ambarelamsiong nena, “A'undi samen, ‘Uahane,’ senenne nenekori a'un haing ubahiogti, ina malingeag poa palamsiogpa, a'undi wana loba nenelamsiog. A'un samen wamlumneap, samen ul palamlumneap kwiripto wail ibare agha liplulom,” aro ambatsiong. 23 Sindi babe a'unag, “Lipmunba a'undi wanaag seneragnange Allah Yameri wendog nembahilul,” aro ambarelamsiong. 24 Nimi wendogne nembamsil nena, elba eldi, “Nimi wendogne sumbahineba, wana porogneap, sikne Nari seneropne sunsunum uropneap mo sol wali uro ulamukang,” aro Allahri nimi wendogne nembamsil. “A'undi samen ul palamlumne kwiripto liapmunba, nimi wendogne nembamsilne sunsunum uropne agha a'un nongag wangaro ul palamsut,” aro amoro ambatsiong.
25 Undop pagha tanena, nu nhin Kristusti nong nhonoghag nembahiog nimi wamap ane sembe, a'un nhin orolenange lilobi agha, yubu sikne aghabog a'un mamun lel dalapmundi, ul dalamlulom. 26 A'un wana yo senelapmunneag agha, malinge uaheng kom. Ik nhon sum wana yo sembamun tanena, heng anam purukan komag sumene agha wana yo senenne neneko lalilamlulom. 27 Wana yo senenne lalilapmun tanena, phia sikini neneko a'un nusamag wa'inebag kolo tatlulom kom. 28 Nimi haoghaba tohiropne wamle tanena, haogha toro ulamlenge neneko liplul. Nen haoghaba toplul kom aghana, eldi walinge agha sol wali uro saeri awe tolamle andarina, nimi kwema ulamsile nimi taneko yepto tarelamsilul.
29 A'un a'unkaboap yubu lelapmun tanena, loba nenehirop yubu a'undi pamag agha lepseng kom. A'un wana walingeag agha nimi eka'uk uropne kemelamsimundi, wana mikip nenelamsimundi, sunsunum urop yubuag agha a'unkaboag lelamlulom. Undo lelapmunne pere agha ka'elamik nimi wali taukang. 30 A'undi yubu lelapmunneri Allah Eldamne Yame Walingeri wana ikin neneropne lepseng kom. Allahri Eldamne Yame Walinge nunag palilamsilneag agha, “Naringe,” senenne kekneba pipsioba, El Yame nunag ya'ag wamle paghabog komopnag sum nu sae lopto taulbahilulne lag talul. 31 A'undi wanaag yonge meso punguropne to, nimiag wana yo senehinne to, nimiag wahinne to, a'undi wanaag ulamsile tanena lipsululom. Nimiag yubu maikno haum aro wahinneap, san tilipsinneap, nimiag yo nenehinneap ni lipsululom. 32 Undopne liapmundi, a'un mamun wana walingeag agha yebel dalapmundi, a'un mamun o'ona senel dalamlulom. A'undi samen malia ulamlum, aghana wene a'un Kristusap ma'al dalamlomne sembe Allahri a'unag wana yorolamsildi, opsin koma tam kom nembaog. Ane saog uro a'un mamun wana yorol dalapmunneag agha a'un mamun tam paip tan koma walia ulamsimundi ulamlulom.
