Allahri Abrahamag, “Me nengabo me nhon tatkinun,” aro salag lebogha (18:1-15)
18
1 Wameka Abraham neneko kal bari Mamre ponekori nubu kal tamag peramag wamogpa, Nuni Allah el whingag lag taog. Ae sumeneko sum ae siro heng pobori ae tomae ina siramag pukamogti 2 whing ebom agha kemelamsiogpa nimi wilindi el whingag sekamek. Sekamekpa tibahiogti Abraham neneko sekom agha sinag merero piogti yabu sanukto sin whingag pulingkina, 3 “Nani yabo, a'undi na sembe o'ona sembamnelomdi yalomba a'epihibom agha peneheng kom. Na a'un arukna wamnari 4 mag tebarel ahineba yan a'elbamundi kal tamag anea tektek eplulom. 5 A'un tektek eplapmunba na kwaneng roti toarel ahinenge timundi mikip tahileba pululom. Nani yabo, nanag yalom ana tektek epna koma pululom kom,” seogpa sindina, “Wah naba nangkae, andi leplamne nene ualulam,” seek.
6 Wene seekpa Abraham neneko elamag-elemag aeag wa'iogti Sara ambarelamogti, “Kwaneng wilindinang maiknoro tukanga sembe sae etparero topmendi winiro taplulam,” aro ambarogti 7 merero pham mo'orobag piogti pham sapi me walinge mesa yagne nhon tobogti, “Elemag oro yobalulam,” aro eldi arukna-ag tarel aogpa elem-elem yobaog. 8 Elem-elem yobaogpa pham sop yobaogne taneko nimi yaek nimi tanekoag pham sop yorobap kwaneng rotiap pham maum mag lolteropneap pham maum agha likto teropneap tarebihiogpa telamekpa el kal eneko tamag sekamog. 9 Sekamogpa sindi yubu lelamekti, “An kel Sara tala wamla?” aro haibaekpa, “Aeag wamla,” aro ambatsiog. 10 Ambatsiogpa sin taneko nhondi yubu lelamogti, “Hengkun nonoag an kel Sara nenekori me mangaro aleri wamleag na samoro anag yanun,” seog.
Wene alamdek abeneko Sara neneko ina siram mombolag sip sin wailag wamogti ka'eamsiog. 11 Abene wamdek li Abrahamap Saraap ya'ag sabal wamdekti Sara babe eneng wal yalamog kom. 12 Undo wamdekti Sara nenekori, “Na kon halo seneoba, na na'angeap ya'ag sabal tanomo aghana, huro na saelbamnelne ponekoap mamnunam?” aro wanaag og solamog. 13 Ogsolamogpa Nuni Allahri Abrahamag hailamogti, “Sa'a sembe Sara wana-ag agha seneraglamlari, ‘Na sabal tanori huro me mangkanun?’ aro ogsobal? 14 A'uni Allahri uanep komne nhon wamla, te? Nari lemna saog uro hengkun nonoag an kel Sara kelenekori me nengabo me mangaro aleba wamtumunag samoro yanun,” aro ambarog. 15 Wene aro ambarogpa Sara nenekori logti, “Ogsoban kom,” aro ipto lebog. Ipto lebogpa Nuni Allahri, “Andi sik uro ogsobalam,” aro ambarog.
Abrahamdi Sodom nimi sembe Allahag molbaogha (18:16-33)
16 Wene aro nimi wilindi abeneko nimi nhondi ambarogpa sin wilindi abeneko lambaekti palamekpa Abrahamdi, “Ina-ag pail pihinun,” aro nhon palamek. Palamekti as Sodom tirobag piekti 17 Nani Allahri eldi wana-ag agha seneraglamogti, “Nari, ‘Uanun,’ senenne Abraham whingag yubu kom tanun kom. 18 Eldi mangkahirobari mangkahyikne maikno taikti, as nubu tangto wamukang. El pere agha Nari nimi sisa ni tala-tala wamikne walingena tatsinun. 19 ‘Eldi maboap sindi mangkahyikneap ambahileba Na yubuog ka'elamikti, sikneog ul palamukang. Sikneog ul palamukang aghana tam waelbaelba, sik uro kareplamukang,’ aro Abrahamog Nari wepsi. Sindi walia aghabog ul palamik tanena Nari salag el neneag pimnonge saog uro ul palamukang,” sembaogti 20 Abrahamag yubu lelamogti, “Sodom Gomora nimiri Na wail ibarelamneangdi yonge aghabog ulamangpa nimi orangeri yubu yanga alamangne Nari ka'elamsin. 21 Ane sembe, ‘Ot sik uro yonge aghabog ulamang to?’ aropne agha kemelingkhinun,” aro ambarog. 22 Ambarogpa nimi yaek nimi abeneko phende Sodom sip pirekpa Nuni Allah aghabog Abrahamap wamdek. 23 Wamdekti Abraham neneko Nuni Allah peramag piogti ambarelamogti, “Nani, Andi, ‘Nimi wali aghabog ulamangneap malia ulamangneap ma'aro opsinun,’ senelamlam? 24 Nimi teng phendea malia urop komne wamik tanena as nene Andi mali nembanep saog, te? ‘Sin tane yo uro wamik,’ aro Sodom nimiri malia ulamangne wailag nenelapmendi mali nembahilulam kom, te? 25 Nimi walia ulamangneap malia ulamangneap ma'aro opsululam kom te? Malia uropnangdi ulamangne agha nimi niag taleag. Nani, Andi ‘uanun’ sembalamne ualulam kom. Andi nimi so'oag wamangne ni sinag tam ua ulamlaba o'olamlamne anekona, sin nimi taneag babe ot sik uro karebalulam, te!” seog.
26 Wene seogpa Nuni Allahri, “As Sodom ane nimi walia ulamangne teng phende wamikpa ipsine tanena, ‘Sinne abene el uro wamik,’ aro Sodom nimiri malia ulamangne wailag nenelamneri mali nembahinun kom,” seog. 27 Seogpa Abraham nenekori nen yubu lelamogti, “Nani, na nimi so'oagne so'o lenglenga saog uro wamnari Anag na yubu lemnep kom saog wamla aghana na yubu paliag uro lemnari wene lemnunne sembe babe yareag sembane. 28 Nimi walia uropne teng phende abeneko lambare orog wamikpa teng nhon eptopne no'op tarabareb wamik tanena lambare abeneko sembe as ni ane opsululam te?” seogpa Nuni Allah ponekori, “Nimi walia urop nimi teng nhon eptopne no'op tarabare wamikpa ipsine tanena lambare abeneko sembe opsinun kom,” seog.
29 Wene seogpa Abraham nenekori nen yubu lelamogti, “Nimi walia uropne teng nhon eptopne naualbare wamik tanena huro senelamlam?” aro haibaogpa Nuni Allahri, “Nimi teng nhon eptopne naualbare wali wamik aro, ‘Uanun,’ sembanenge lipsinun,” seog.
30 Wene seogpa nen Abrahamdi, “Nani, nen nhon lemnunne sembe wabaneheng kom! Nimi walia uropne nimi teng nhon eptopne wilindi wamik tanena wela ualulam?” seogpa Nuni Allahri, “Sin teng nhon eptopne wilindiog wamikpa ipsine tanena nari, ‘Uanun,’ sembanena uanun kom,” seog.
31 Wene seogpa Abrahamdi nen ambarelamogti, “Nani, Anag yubu lemnep kom saog wamla aghana yubu pam keyagman menanga lemnari nen lemnunne sembe yo sembaneheng kom. Nimi walia uropne lin tarabareog wamik tanena huro ualulam?” seogpa Nuni Allahri, “Nimi ot lin tarabareog wamikpa ipsine tanena as ane orog nembanun kom,” seog.
32 Wene seogpa nen Abrahamdi ambarelamogti, “Nani, wana mali sembaheng kom! Ot nhon babe lemnun. Ot nimi sa'obareog wamik tanena as ane mali nembalulam te?” aro ambarogpa Nuni Allahri, “Ot nimi sa'obareog wamikpa ipsine tanena as ane orog nembanun kom,” seog.
33 Yubu komopto leptekti Allah babe piogpa Abraham elenge aeag piog.
