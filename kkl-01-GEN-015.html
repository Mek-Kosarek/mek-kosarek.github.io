Allahri Abramag salag pibogha (15:1-21)
15
1 Wamogpa Abram nenekori haing uamna kwelekamna senenag agha uro yabinepne kemelamogpa Nuni Allahri Abramag yubu lelamogti, “Abram, log sembaheng kom! ‘Malinge anag taleag,’ aro Nari an piangkeneri kamna nubunge tatkinun,” seog. 2-3 Wene seogpa Abramdi yubu lelamogti, “Nani Allah sa'a aba agha Andi na tatnululam, Nani na me nhon tatneom komba na me koma tebaneba naringe sae agha nari arukna Eliezer Damsyikne ponekoag talul,” seog. 4 Wene seogpa Allahri yubu lelamogti, “Nimi etneag babe andinge sae agha elag talul kom. Ot andi me taptopneag andinge sae agha elag talul,” seogti 5 Abram enero asag poa lambaogti ambarelamogti, “Imag tiwalengkipmendi imberea tiwalengkiplulam! Imberea a'ilbanep kom wamla saog uro andi mangarobari mangkahyikne taneko maikno taukang,” seog. 6 Allahri lebogne nene sembe Abramdi, “Ot sik uro lepla,” aro wana paiamogpa, Nuni Allahri, “El wana porogne,” sembaog.
7 Kwelekaogpa, nen Abramag Allahri yubu lelamogti, “Na Nani Allah wamna. ‘So'o ane an ngai talulam,’ aro Nari Ur-Kasdim so'oag agha enero payaghai,” seog.
8 Wene seogpa Abramdi Allahag hailamogti, “Nani Allah nubunge, Andi, ‘Mog so'o ane tatkinun,’ selam aghana, kekneba uamenba ‘Sik uro lepla,’ sembanun,” aro haibaog. 9 Haibaogpa Nuni Allahri, “Pham sapi kelne nhon, kambing kelne nhon, domba yemne nhon hengkun wilindi ubu piamangneap winang mem winang ologne nhon, nubunge nhon tarel anululam,” seog. 10 “Tarel anululam,” seogpa payaogti pham taneko usoghag agha amuag serelingkiogti lelero palilamogti winang abeneko ningki oro pibog. 11 Pibogti wamogpa winang pham kalerop winang yingkina tukap aro ulamekpa Abramdi yaglamsiog. 12 Heng purukanep peram ulamogpa Abram kwhaingti mangkhauk mabog abeneko inipna tuka'ag aropne logne niknilaog. 13 Tuka'ag seog abeneko Allah yubu lelamogti, “El uro wamlulam. Andi mangaroba mangarobari mangkahyikne yog nimiri so'oag nimi sekelanan nang wamukang. Hengkun teng naual tarabare saog ulamlea (400) yog nimiri so'oag ua ulamikpa, so'o enekoag wamik nimiri sin oro aruklamsiikpa, ikinaba maghan og uro polamukang.a 14 Aghana sin arukna nembamsiik nimi sisa taneko nari tam karebi agha lunuro opsinun. Opsineba anisag yabo lambaikti, wali-waliaba maikno poro agha lambaukang. 15 An aghabogna anenge nimi sisa likna pimenba, hong kul wangkeleba, wana aingniro tebamenba, so'oag sikkukang. 16 Aghana nimi sisa andinge nimi likna tomne neneko samoro ane yaukang. Amori nimi mog so'o ngai nimi yonge aghabog ulamangne Na wana sesog wamnel kom. Wana sesog uanele sum lumbanun,” seog. 17 Heng purukaogti imbaogpa Abramdi kemelamogpa auk una-ap whea-ap pham kalero phendeag piroba anabiag auk una mhong yarut alamogpa whea auk pog tagtag alamogpa yabalamog. 18-21 Undo unulamog abeneko Allahri sumene Abramag salag lelamogti, “Andi mabo mangaroba-ag mog so'o nene anag Mesira yalamlari mag nubunge Efrat neneag. Keni nimiri so'oap, Kenas nimiri so'oap, Kadmon nimiri so'oap, Het nimiri so'oap, Feris nimiri so'oap, Rafaim nimiri so'oap, Amori nimiri so'oap, Kanaan nimiri so'oap, Girgasi nimiri so'oap, Yebus nimiri so'oap andi mangaroba-ag tatsinun,” seog.b
