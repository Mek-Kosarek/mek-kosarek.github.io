Ae Mororo Sopso Walelamekpa Allahri Obsiogha
11
1 Samen nimi so'oag wamekne taneko niri yubu ot nhonog yubuog lelamek. 2 Sin taneko yale heng walelingkirobag sip piekti mog so'o pulu nhon Sinear ibekti so'o eneag wamek. 3 Wamekti sin-sin yubu lelamekti, “Ae nhon sobaukap,” aro yubu lelamekti, “So'o winiro aukag tabebeba kirik saog taleba kirik pai so'o paia ubu walelameberi sobukap,” seekti so'o winiro tabekti 4 lelamekti, “Nun sembe wamukapag as nhon ya'aro solameberi nusamag ae nhon mororo sopso im tamag solingkukap. Ae ene sobaberi, nun dam nimi si nubu nenel dalameberi, talup tangto kom-kom wamukap,” seek. 5 Wene aro seneraghekti solamekpa Nuni Allahri kemelingkihinun aro kulukul aog. 6 Kulukul aogti Nuni Allah ponekori, “Wene nimi mog so'o eneag wamangne yubu ot nhon, nimi mog so'o as eneag wamangne babe ot nhon. Wene alnirobag alnibang tanena, amik wamikti phelektopne ulamukang. 7 Ane sembe Na kulukaneri yubu yog-yog nembahineba nhondi nhon yubu ka'ebanep kom wamlul,” seog. 8 Wene seogti Nuni Allahri, “Sin unulamangne tanena mog so'oag tala-tala taik,” aro talup lambahiogpa, as solamekne eneko soban koma laploa tala-tala taek. 9 Nuni Allahri nimi yubu mo-mo nenelamsiogti, talup lambahiogpa, tala-tala taekag aneko si “Babel”b engkaog.
Sem elmabo mangaro piogne
10 Sem elmabo ane. Sem hengkun-kunne teng wilindi epto ko'olom tarabare (98) wamogpa magti opsiog. Aneko nen hengkun phende wamogti Sem mangaro aogne teng wilindi epto supna tarabare (100) wamogpa el yandome si Arpakhsad mangaro aog. 11 Mangaro aogti me nenneap kelmabo nenneap mangkahiogti, Sem wamogne hengkun-kunne teng no'op tarabare epto no'opbare (600) wamogti tebaog.
12 Sem elme Arpakhsad hengkun teng nhon epto linbare (35) wamogti me si Selah mangaro aog. 13 Selah mangaro aogti me nenneap kelmabo nenneap mangkahiogti Arpakhsad wamogne hengkun-kunne teng ao tarabare epto no'opbare (438) wamogti, tebaog.
14 Arpakhsad elme Selah hengkun teng nhon epto wilindi (30) wamogti me si Eber mangaro aog. 15 Eber mangaro aogti me nenneap kelmabo nenneap mangkahiogti Selah wamogne hengkun-kunne teng ao tarabare epto selek (433) wamogti, tebaog.
16 Selah elme Eber hengkun teng nhon epto saekpare (34) wamogti me si Peleg mangaro aog. 17 Peleg mangaro aogti me nenneap kelmabo nenneap mangkahiogti Eber wamogne hengkun-kunne teng ko'olom tarabare epto lambare (464) wamogti tebaog.
18 Eber elme Peleg wamogne hengkun teng nhon epto wilindi (30) wamogti me si Rehu mangaro aog. 19 Rehu mangaro aogti me nenneap kelmabo nenneap mangkahiogti Peleg wamogne hengkun-kunne teng linbare epto lam tarabare (239) wamogti, tebaog.
20 Peleg elme Rehu hengkun teng nhon epto lambare (32) wamogti Serug mangaro aog. 21 Serug mangaro aogti me nenneap kelmabo nenneap mangkahiogti Rehu wamogne hengkun-kunne teng linbare epto lam tarabare (239) wamogti tebaog.
22 Rehu elme Serug hengkun teng nhon epto wilindi (30) wamogti me si Nahor mangaro aog. 23 Nahor mangaro aogti me nenneap kelmabo nenneap mangkahiogti Serug wamogne hengkun-kunne teng linbare epto mekpare (230) wamogti, tebaog.
24 Serug elme Nahor hengkun teng nhon epto phende (29) wamogti me si Terah mangaro aog. 25 Terah mangaro aogti me nenneap kelmabo nenneap mangkahiogti Nahor wamogne hengkun-kunne teng lambare epto naualbare (148) wamogti tebaog.
26 Nahor elme Terah hengkun-kunne teng phende epto ao tarabare (70) wamogti me si Abramap, Nahorap, Haranap mangkahiog.
 
Terah elmabo sembe lebogha (11:27-32)
27 Terah elmabo si tane wamla. Terahri me wilindi mangkahiogne Abram, Nahor, apna Haranap. Harandi Lot mangaro aogti nen elme si nhon Yiska mangaro aog. 28 Haran neneko ili kamag wamogpa elenge as Ur-Kasdima tebaog. 29 Abram nenekori elkel Sarai toro aogpa Nahor nenekori Haran elmangkel Milka toro aog. 30 Sarai neneko ibili talamogti me nhon mangaro an koma wamog. 31 Terah nenekori elme Haran mangaro aogne Lotap, Abram elkel Saraiap, enero, “Ur-Kasdim so'o eneko laplobi agha as Kanaan pukap,” sembaek aghana as Kharan piekti wamek. 32 Pina wamekti Terah mangkaekne hengkun teng saekpare eptopne ao tarabare (205) wamogti Terah mog so'o Kharan agha tebaog.
