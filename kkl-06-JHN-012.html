Kel nhondi Yesusag song wali mag ikto pelaghaogha
(Matius 26:6-13; Markus 14:3-9)
12
1 Wameka aneko, “Ik no'opbare wameberi, Paskah sum seneragto ul palamukap,” aro wamek sum enekoag Yesus as Betania piog. As Betania anekona, Lazarus el teptoba agha kamag nembaogne poneko wamoghag Yesus piog. 2 “Yesus nunag yal,” senenne sembe, sindi el toro pibekpa, Martari kwaneng yoro yeplamsiog. Sin Yesusap pulingkina kwaneng nikag telamekne nhon Lazarus babe wamog. 3 Yesusap kwaneng nhon telamekpa, Maria kel enekori mag song walinge kelang purunne nhon payaog. Payaog nena, kamna kaptoba alikin sirik uro toropne nongag pelagtop mag song walinge payaogti, Yesusti yan sangag ikto pelagto eldi usog hong agha lubog. Ikto pelaghaogpa, mag song walinge enekori song ae lom eneko to'op taog.
4 Undo uaog aghana, Yesusti yubu ka'ero neleptopne nhon Yudas Iskariotne Yesus pan khebanepne nenekori yubu lelamogti, 5 “Song wali mag enena, nimiri hengkun nhon awe welamnaba, kamna elag tatnepneag saog uro kamna kapto tomnep mag wamla. Kel eneri nimiag kamna kapto topsori, nimi saeagha orog nimiag tatsinep. Wene ualne sa'a sembe ual?” aro wabaog.
6 Eldi wene seog nena, “Nimi saeagha orogne tatsisene,” aro lebog kom aghana, el haogha toropne wamogti, “Topsene,” aro lebog. Eldi haogha tomnep nena, nimiri yepsinne tatsiroba eldi saeag nenelamekpa, paia unne wamog aghana, haogha toro telamog.
7 Aghana Yesusti yubu lelamogti, “Kel eneri ualne lipsut. Na tebaneba so'oag siknukangne sembe ual. 8 Nimi saeagha orog nimina, a'unap sop-sop ua ulamukang aghana, na sop-sop a'unap wamnun kom,” seog.
“Lazarus obukap” aro yubu saekag lebeka
9 Yahudi nimi maiknori ka'elamekpa, “Yesus as Betania wamla,” seeka ka'ebaekti, Betania aneko piek. Piek nena, Yesus aghabog kemel piek kom aghana, eldi nimi teptoba agha kamag nembaogne poneko babe, “Lazarus kembaukap,” aro piek. 10-11 Ot Lazarus poneko sembena, Yesusti el kamag nembaogha sembe nimi maiknori Yahudi nimi nubu nang lilobi agha Yesusag sikne aro seneraglamek. Ane sembe, memnang Allah sembe palirop nang sikini yabori, “Lazarus babe obukap,” sembaek.
Yesus nimiri kiplamekne toman agha as Yerusalem wa'iogha
(Matius 21:1-11; Markus 11:1-11; Lukas 19:28-40)
12 Yahudi nimiri ik nubu sum Paskah ul pinepne sembe nimi maikno as Yerusalem yingkina wamekti ka'elamekpa, “Yesus ‘Yerusalem pinun,’ aro ulamlari, inaag yalamla,” alameka ka'ebaek. 13 Ka'ebaekti tol ko ma'alap tobekti, Yesus yalamnep inaag la'iniro sekel ingkina yubu mikip uro kiplamekti,
“Nu Taulba Uhiropne kiplamsut!
Nu Saelba Uhiropneri poglaba, el siag kekhaekto yalamlange poneko
nu Israel nimi sembe yubu sia lenne paliag uropne omeklamsut!”
aro haum aro kiplamek.
14 Yesus pham keledai me nhon ibogti, pham alibag waelbaogti pulingkiog. Pham alibag pulingkiogha aneko Allahri yubu mome toro pairopneag sunsunum uro taog nena,
15 “A'un Sion wamlom nimi nari kelme saog wamloma nimi log sembaheng kom!
A'un sembe yubu sia lenne poneko a'unag yalamlange kembahut!
Pham keledai me nhon alibag pukamlari yalamla,”
aro mome toro pibog.
16 Undo Allah yubu sunsunum uro yabalamogne sembe Yesusti yubu ka'ero neleplamek nimi tanekori samenag olog seneragamek kom. Ot Yesus amik tebaogti, kamag tangom agha paliag taog taneogna, sindi babe seneraghek. Amik sum el taek nena, “Ni uro yabalamogne neneko Allahri yubu tiptopneri anamag mome toro pibogpa, amik sum elag undo unuaek,” aro amik sum el taek.
17 Sum eneko longoro kiplamek nimi nusamag wamek nimina, samenag Yesusti Lazarus kamag nembaog sum elap nhon wamekti ibek. Sin nimi tanekori ora nimi sikne lag nenero ambarelamsiekti, “Lazarus tebaoba, kirik lomag wirikil piong aghana Yesusti elag, ‘Lambae,’ aro yobonge toman agha kamag nembao. Kamag nembaoba, lambaoa ibobo,” aro ambarelamsiek. 18 “Yesusti Allah mikibari uanepne kekneba nene uao,” alameka ka'ebaekne sembe nimi maikno, “El kemel pukap,” aro elag longoro yalamek. 19 Ane sembe Farisi nimiri sin mamun yubu lelamekti, “Kembamsut! Wene ulamapneri nundam nimi yebel dalamap kom. Mog so'oag nimi ni elag nelepto nunag agha lanalamang,” aro wene alamek.
Yunani nimi nhon-nhondi, “Yesusap haiptahabe,” aro elameka
20 Sin waelbaek nimi, “Ik nubu sum Paskah ulameberi, Allah omeklamukap,” aro wamek nimi nusamag nimina, Yunani nimi etpare babe waelbaek. 21 Waelbaekti Filipus ibek. Filipus Galilea so'oag wamogne as Betsaidange ponekoag yingkina mololamekti, “Nai, nu Yesusap haiptahabe senelamap,” aro molbaek.
22 Wene aro molbaekpa, Filipusti yubu eneko poa lebogti, Andreas ambarel piog. Ambarogpa sin phenderi yubu eneko poa leptekti, Yesusag ambattek.
23 Ambattekpa ka'ebaogti Yesusti sin phendeag ambarelamsiogti, “Wene na mog so'oag nimi tangtopne paliag nembaneika wene uro yabinual. 24 Nari yubu sikne agha ambatnuahin. Gandum wana nhon tebanep kom tanena, nhonog wamnep. Aghana gandum wana nhon so'oag togna agha tebale taneogna, pongkaleri maikno yabinep. 25 Eldamne kamag wamnepne o'omne senerop nimiri kamag uro unne neneko loba nenelamla. Apna eldamne mog so'o eneag kamag wamnepne o'omne senerop kom nimina, kamag uro unne elag wamlange amik ka'eseptan koma ya'ag uro elag wamlul. 26 Nimi etne na sembe, ‘Yeplamnun,’ sembale tanena, nanag neleplamnelul. Apna na wamneag aneko na arukna yepnerop nimi eneko babe wamlul. Na yeplamnele nimi eneko sembe na Naniri el kipto kamna tatlul,” aro ambatsiog.
Yesus el tebanepne sembe ambatsiogha
27 Yesusti nen tipto yubu lelamogti, “Wene na wana samsamolamnelba, nari sa'a yubu agha lemnep? Nari Naniag, ‘Nanag uro yabilulne nene angkitlulam,’ anne babe sunsunum kom. Aghana na yahingena, nanag uro yabinep ko'o ene, ‘Uro yabilul,’ aro yahi. 28 Nai, andi si nubunge paliag uropne lag nembalulam,” seog.
Wene seogpa, imag agha yubu lelamogti, “Nari si nubunge paliag wamlange lag nembanoba, nen babe lag nembanun,” alamogha ka'ebaog. 29 Nimi maikno taneko wamek nimiri babe yubu wene alamog yubu lilim eneko ka'ebaek. Ka'ebaekti sindi yubu lelamekti, “Limang yubu agha lepla,” alamek. Aghana nenneri lelamekti, “Limang yubu kom. Allah arukna imagne malaikatti elag yubu lepla,” alamek.
30 Sindi wene alamekpa, Yesusti sinag yubu lelamogti, “Yubu lilim ka'ebalomne ene na sembe lepla kom aghana, a'un el talulom aro lepla. 31 Wene wamap ko'o enena, mog so'oag nimiri ul piropne sembe tam karebanepne wamla. Mog so'oag nimi saelbamsil phia neneko Allahri wene luknuro yag lambalul. 32 Na mog so'o eneag agha alikinag nembaneik tanena, nari nimi niag, ‘Nanag yalulom,’ aro kiliro yoplamsinun,” seog. 33 Eldi wene, “Alikinag nembanukang,” seog nena, nimiri el kalag sipto omnaba, tebanepne sembe lebog.
34 Wene seogpa, nimi maikno longolamek nimi tanekori elag samoro yubu lelamekti, “Allahri aruksirop yubu lelamlonga ka'eamapne sunsunum uro Kristus ponekona, ‘Ya'ag wamlul,’ alamonga ka'eamap. Undop pagha tanena andi, ‘Mog so'oag nimi tangtopne poneko alikinag nembanepneog wamla,’ selamne neneko sa'ange agha leplam? ‘Mog so'oag nimi tangtopne,’ selamne poneko etne sembe leplam?” aro haibaek.
35 Wene aro haibaekpa Yesusti, “Ae tibin aneko a'unag warerop ko'o we koma wamnual. Nimi inibinag yabirop nimi eneko, ‘Tala pinun?’ aro ina erop. Ane sembe ae tibin aneko a'unag wene warelamlange abeneko a'undi, ‘Inibinne nenekori nunag niknibahileag,’ aro yabalamlulom. 36 Tibin a'unag wareropne abeneko tibin anekoag sikne aro wana palilamlulom andarina, a'unag tibin wareropne neneri a'un Allah elmabo nembahilul,” aro ambatsiog.
Yesusti wene seogti, el la'imbom agha anekoa piogti, “Sin whingag wamnun kom,” aro tognop taog.
Yahudi nimiri sik senelamek komne
37 Yesusti samenag Allah mikibari uanepne kekneba maikno sindi whingag agha ulamog aghana, sindi elag sikne aro seneraglamek kom. 38 Sindi sik senelamek komne uro yabinep andarina, Allahri yubu silimu tiptopne Yesayari samenag mome toro pibog yubu sunup tanep. Eldi samenag mome toro pibog yubuna,
“Nai, nu Saelba Uhiropne, nuri ambatsibo yubu sikne aro seneragtop nimi nhon babe wamla te?
Apna nu Saelba Uhiropneri sae mikipne neneko andi nimiag lag nembatsiom kom te?”
aro mome toro samenag pibog. 39 Ane sembe sindi sikne aro seneragnep kom uro wamek. Nen nhon babe Yesayari tam nene sembe mome toro pibog nena,
40 “Allahrina, ‘Nanag samoro yahengpa, wali nembahisene,’ seog aghana,
eldi sindi haing ulopsiori, sindi wana kas nenelopsio.
Andarina sindi haingdi imnepne babe,
wanaag seneragnepne babe orogti,
elag samoro yanep kom,”
aro Yesayari mome toro pibog. 41 Wene seog nena, Yesus amik paliag nenero kipnepne Yesayari ibogti, Yesus mog so'o eneag anam mangap tan komag el sembe lebog.
42-43 Undop aghana, Yahudi nimi maiknori babe, sin nubu nang nhon-nhondi babe Yesusag sikne aro seneraglamek. Ot sin nimi maiknori Allahri paliag nenero kipsinepne semban koma, “Nimiri na paliag nenero kipnukang,” senenne agha o'omne nenelamek. Ane sembe Farisi nimi sembe sin logti, “Allah omekto winirop aeag agha na yag lambaneikag,” aro nimi maikno tanekori Yesusag sik senelamekne lag phoro lelamek kom.
Yesusti ambarelamsiog yubu toman agha tam karebatsilula
44 Yesusti nen yubu mikip uro ambarelamsiogti, “Nanag sikne aro seneragtop nimi eneko nanag mo sikne aro seneragtop kom aghana, na pogneogne ponekoag sikne aro seneraglamla. 45 Nimi enekori na kemelamnelne toman agha na pogneogne poneko babe ililamla. 46 Na mog so'oag yahingena, ‘Tibin warebahinun,’ aro yahi andarina, nanag sikne aro seneragtop nimi ni inibinag wamukang kom.
47 Nimi nhondina, nari ambarelamsin yubu ene ka'ebi agha saelban koma wamnep. Na mog so'oag nimiag, ‘Tam karepto pelengkatsinun,’ aro yahi kom aghana, ‘Mog so'oag nimi taulbahinun,’ aro yahi. Ane sembe nari yubu saelban koma wamnep nimi enekoag tam karepto pelengelamsin kom. 48 Nari yubu saelban koma wa'epto epnele nimi eneko sembe elag tam karepto pelengkimnepne nhon wamla. Nari lelamna yubu eneri nimi eneko sembe ik komopnag sum tam karebaleri, elag pelengkiplul. 49 Nari wanaag senenne agha yubu mo ambatsin kom aghana, na Naniri pogneogne ponekori arukamneogne agha ambarelamsin. Eldi nanag, ‘Yubu ene agha lepmendi, undop yubu agha ambatsululam,’ aro ambarelamneog. 50 Apna na el wamnangena, eldi aruksirop yubu eneko nimi ya'ag uro kamag nenehinne wamla. Ane sembe nari lelamna yubu eneko ot Naniri nanag ambareamneo yubu sunsunum uro lelamna,” aro Yesusti ambatsiog.
