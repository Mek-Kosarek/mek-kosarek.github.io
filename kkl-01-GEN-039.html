Yusupap Potifarti kelap sembe lebogha (39:1-23)
39
1 Ismael nimiri Yusup neneko Mesir pabiekti, Potifar tarel piekpa, eldi kamna tatsiog. Potifar neneko Mesir nimi sikini yae wamek nang yin saleropne sikindo wamog. 2 Yusup neneko ili Mesirne ponekoap wamogpa, NUNI Allahri yeplamogpa, awe wali uro welamog. 3 Wamogne Potifar nenekori kemelamogpa, Ili Allahri me aneko yeplamogpa, “Awe ambarelamnange ni awe wali uro welamla,” aro kemelamog. 4 Undo ulamogha kembaogti, Potifar nenekori Yusup sembe o'ona senelamogti, “An agha na peramag wapmendi, yeplamnululam,” aro ambarelamog. Nen nhon ambarelamogti, “Na naringe aeag wamlange naringe saeag agha ni andi o'olamlulam,” aro sekirog.
5 Toro sekirogti, wamogpa, NUNI Allahri Yusubag seneraglamogti, Potifarti ae eneko wali nenelamogpa, Potifar ponekoringe aeag wamogne paiag wamogne ni wali uro tolamog. 6 Nia mangkina eldinge taneko Yusupti saeag pibogti, eldamneri olog uan koma, ot tena aghabog seneraglamog.
Yusup neneko kabuni kemerop wamog. 7 Wamekti, Potifarti keldi Yusup kembi agha wana telamogpa, lebogti, “An nanap mamnunamdi yae,” seog.
8 Wene seogpa, Yusupti, “Karong,” aro yubu lelamogti, “Nanin, nani Potifar nenekori nia mangkina eldinge aeag wamlange, ‘Andi piamlulam,’ aro ambatneori, nia mangkina o'ol tolamla kom. 9 Naniri nia mangkina sembe ambarelamsilba, ka'elamang saog uro nari babe ambarelamsinba ka'ebanep. Nia mangkina ni ae eneag nari saeag pibog aghana, nanin, na nani elkel wamlam ana an sembe naniri ambatneo kom. An sembe ambatneo komba, welaro nari malia ulamneri, Allah wana loba nembanun?” aro ambarog.
10 Wene aro ambarogti, wamogpa, kelenekori sop-sop yubu yangaro, “Nhon mamnunam,” alamog aghana Yusup ka'elamog kom.
11 Ik nhon sum ili arukna nenne oroghag Yusup aeag wa'iogti, aeag wamogha “O'obanun,” aro aeag wa'iog. 12 Aeag wa'iogti, aeag wamogha “O'obanun,” aro ulamoghag agha ili Potifar elkel wa'alulum agha Yusupti ag enekto amogneag saelbaogti, “Wene nhon mamnunam,” aro wene alamoghag agha Yusup, “Karong,” aro ag enekto amogne neneko kwiripto keleneko saeag tan aogha laplobi agha lahimbom seog. 13 Yusupti ag eneko el saeag taogha kembi agha 14 kelenekori eldi arukna yopsiogti, ambarelamsiogti, “Namabo, na na'angeri Ibraninge ae eneag tolongol aoba, yubu neplamsil. El neneko na marobag wa'ildi, ‘Nanap mamnunam,’ aro ulamlaba, na ngolol alamna. 15 Ngolol alamna sembe logti, lanalamlari elenge ag laplobi agha pilne kembahut!” aro ambatsiog.
16 Wene aro ambatsiogti, kelenekori ag aneko piamogti, wamogpa, ela'ange yaogpa, 17-18 ambarelamogti, “Ibraninge andi arukna payaomne ponekori aeag wa'ildi, na ‘Saelbanun,’ aro ulamnelag agha nari yubu ngolol aro, haum alamnange sembe log lelem palamlari, ag kwiripto liloa pilne ane,” aro ambarog.
19 Wene aro ambarogpa, ela'ange Potifar nenekori ka'ebaogti, wanaag yo sembaog. 20 Yo sembaogti, Yusup neneko salero Potifarti kop aeag tolongol piog. Kop ae tolongol pioghag aneko nimiri Mesir yubu sia leropneag malia uanba, tolongol pirob aeag tolongol piek. 21 Tolongol piekpa, NUNI Allahri Yusup yeplamogpa, kop ae yae ua uropne ponekori Yusup sembe o'ona senelamog. 22 O'ona senelamogti, “Nimi orange haing sae kaupto tolongolamsiekne andi kemelamsimendi, sa'a-sa'a kop aeag uropne andi o'olamlulam,” aro ambarog. 23 Ambarogti, kemelamogpa, Yusup neneko Ili Allahri yeplamogpa, awe eldi welamogne sikneog uro yabalamogpa, kembaogti, Yusup saeag awe pibogne taneko tolamoghag kemel palamog kom.
