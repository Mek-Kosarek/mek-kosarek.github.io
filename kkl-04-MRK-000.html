Markus
PONEKORI YUBU WALINGE YESUS SEMBE
MOME TOBOG YUBU
Markusti mome tobogne sembe tibin nenerop yubu palilamap
Markus Yubu ane Yohanes-Markus as Yerusalemneri mome tobog. Yohanes-Markus poneko Petrus, Yesus neleptopne, elkae wamog. Wamogti Petrusti Yesus sembe ambarelamsiogne ka'ebaogti, mome tobog (Pogsiog Nang 12:12, 25).
Markusti mome toro pibog nena, “Yesus Allah Elme wamogne poneko mog so'oag ua ulamogne sembe babe, ‘Mog so'oag nimi taulbahinun,’ aro seklero tebaogne sembe babe el nembahinun,” aro Markusti mome toro pibog. Yubu eneagna, Yesusti Allahri mikipne elag pibogne toman agha nimi maikno wali nenelamsiogne sembeap, Allah yubu ambarelamsiogne sembeap, phia yag lanalamsiogne sembeap, nimiri mali-malia uropne tam wailag nenelamogne sembeap Markusti mome toro pibog.
Markusti mome ene komoptobag wamlange, kekneba leka saogne ene [...] Markusti yubu 16:9-20 abolag pibapne nene, “Lomag mome samenag sirik momeag wamog kom,” aro el nenero pibap. Markus ponekori samenag mome toro pibog yubuna, ora nimiri amik sum eldi momeag nelepto, “Nen sarikto mome toro pibeberi, sabo nembaukap,” aro ulamek. Nimi nhon-nhondi, “Sabo nembaukap,” aro mome toro palilamekne abeneko yubu komoptobag leka lomag wamla mome nene ma'aro pibek. Samenag sirik momeag yubu nene orog, aghana amik mome komoptobag wamlange ma'aro pibek.
Markusti mome tobog yubu tam saekpare karebi agha a'ero-a'ero palilamap
1:1-13 Allahri Elme Yesus wepto poghogne lag taogha
1:14--9:50 Yesus mog so'o Galilea wamogha Allah yubu ambarelamsiogti, nimi wali nenelamsiogha
10:1-52 Yesus mog so'o Galilea laplobi agha as Yerusalem sip palamogha
11:1--12:44 Yesus as Yerusalem wa'ina Allahri Mem Ae Allah yubu ambarelamsiogha
13:1-37 Yesusti tam amik uro yabilulne sembe ambarelamsiogha
14:1--15:47 Yesus seklebaogti, tebaogha
16:1-20 Yesus nen kamag taogti, imag yingkiogha
