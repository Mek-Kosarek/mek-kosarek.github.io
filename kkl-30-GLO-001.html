Daud elme Sin Yahudi nimi agha Musari yubu amohirop nangdi samenag ambarelamsiekne, “Allahri ‘Saelbamsululam,’ aro wepto pogtopne Kristus poneko Daud sisange mangaptalul ane sembe, ‘Daud elme,’ aro engeropne yabalamla,” aro ambarelamsiek. Markusti mome toro pibog yubu 12:35 kembanep.

mikibari uanepne Mog so'oag nimiri uanepne komne, aghana nimi nhondi uan tanena, ora nimiri, “Memne mikibari el pere agha ual,” sembanep
Musari yubu amohirop nang Allahri samenag Israel nimiag, “Yubu nikne lebaukap,” seogti eldi aruksirop yubu Musa pere agha sinag ambatsiog. Amik sum Musari yubu amohirop nang tanekori yubu eneko yubu kiki telekto el talamekti, Israel nimiag amoro ambarelamsiek.
Paskah Sin Yahudi nimi Mesir so'oag wamekpa, Mesir nimiri sin arikal aro aruklamsiekpa, seklero wamek. Allahri sin amik sum Yahudi nimi Mesir so'oag agha enero poa yag pabihiogne sembe yeng uro seneragto ulamekti, wene babe pham domba oro telamang sum eneko sembe “Paskah sum,” aro si engero yabalamla. Allahri Israel nimi ambatsiogne, Memnang 23:5-6 ag agha mome tolamogne ane, “Wal selekne, tanggal 14 sumeneko heng purukanebag alnilamlulom nena, Allahri Israel nimi opsin koma Mesir nimi opsiogha sembe seneraglapmundi, Allah omeklamlulom. Kwelekalea, tanggal 15 sumeneko kwaneng roti walerop komne teropne alnibamunna, ik saekpare tero wapmuna, tanggal 21 sumeneko tero komoplamlulom,” aro mome toro pibog. Wal selekne tanggal 14 sumeneko, Indonesia yubuag “Paskah” alamang. Yahudi nimiri “wal selekne” alamangneap, nuri “wal selekne” alamapneap, nika-nika kom. Yahudi nimiri “wal selekne sina Nisan” alamangba, wilindip wal Maret anabi tom wal April anabiag uro yabalamla.
Korintus nimiag Paulusti mome toro palilamogti, “Kristus poneko nu sembe tebaog nena, Yahudi nimiri, “Allahri nu opsin koma taulbahiog,” aro Paskah sum pham domba oro telamek saog uro Kristus poneko nu sembe pham domba saog tebaog,” aro mome toro pibog (1 Kor 5:7). Wene nu Kristusag wana palirop nimi sembe, “Paskah,” aro yubu lelamap nena, “Allahri nu opsin koma talulamsil,” aro Kristus tebaogti, kamag taogne sembe seneraglamap.
Roma nimi sembe kamna poloro torop nang Roma nimi sembe kamna poloro torop nang tanekona, saelba uhiropnangdi yubu eptopnang wamek. Kamna poloro torop nangdi sin mog so'o aneko wamek nimiag, “Kamna tolamlomne agha saelba uhirop nang sembe pogto nanag palilamlulom,” aro aruklamsiek. Aruklamsiekpa payalamekpa, kamna kaptoba neneko sikini yabo saeag tipto tarelamsiek. Mog so'o aneko wamek nimi sunsunum uro kamna payingkina tatsiek kom tanena, yin salerop nangdi nimi taneko salero haing sae kaupto kop aeag tobongkipsinep.
