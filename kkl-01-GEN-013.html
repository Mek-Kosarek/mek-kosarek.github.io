Abramap Lotap ina a'ep tareka (13:1-13)
13
1 Abram babe Lot babe sikinenge aba sikin kelabo toro agha Mesir so'oag agha Negeb sip palamek. 2 Sin pina wamekti, pham Abramdinge maikno taogpa, wali-walia leng-leng aroba kirik emas perak wamogpa, tupne wamog. 3-4 Inaghag maba palamogti, Negeb so'oag agha Betel piog. Piogti Betelap Aiap anabiag samenag, “Allahag mhong towalengkanun,” aro kirik yangaro piboghag aneko wamogti, Nuni Allah omeklamog. 5 Abramap Lotap palamdekne abeneko Lotdinge aruknaap, pham domba, kambing, sapiap, ae kalun aeap maikno wamog. 6-7 Wamogpa, abene li Kanaan nimiap Feris nimiap mog so'o aneag babe wamek. Undo wamekti, sindinge pham maikno wamogpa sindinge pham paia unag sunsunum kom wamog. Sin nikag wamnep kom saog wamog. Abramdi pham yae ua urop nimiap Lotdi pham yae ua urop nimiap ol dalamekpa 8 Abram nenekori Lotag yubu lelamogti, “Anap nanap andi phamap, nari phamap, paia yae ua urop nangap tana sunsunum komdi nunna elkae-elkae. 9 Ane sembe mog so'o ane sabo wamlaba an babe mo tamenba na babe mo tanun. An waneng sip tanamen tanena na sirik sip tanun. An sirik sip tanamen tanena na waneng sip tanun,” seog. 10 Wene seogpa Lot whing kanebom agha tilamogpa Yordan sip Zoar ina agha mag lu wali wamogti samen Allahri Eden so'o walinge sumbaogne neneko saog wamogha tibog aog. Nene unu arekne nene anam Nuni Allahri Sodom nimiap Gomora nimiap opsin komag uarek. Mog so'o unu arekne nene samen Mesir mog so'oag sukuptopne walinge sa'a nia mangkina wamogne saog mog so'o wamog. 11 Undo wamogpa Lotti tibaogti, “Mag Yordan sip so'o pulu waliag na ngai tanun,” aro Abram laplobi agha heng walelingkirobag sip piogti wamog. 12 Abram Kanaan so'oag wamogpa Lot mag Yordan pulu waliag wamogti ae soro Sodom peramag wamog. 13 As Sodom wamek nimi tanekori Nuni Allah wail ibarelamekti yonge aghabog maikno ulamek.
Abram Hebron ko'oro piogha (13:14-18)
14 Lot neneko, “A'ero wamnunam,” aro piogpa Allah Nuni ponekori Abramag yubu lelamogti, “Sekamlamag anea mog so'o tibalulam, heng walelingkirobag sip, purukul pirobag sip, mag luag sip, kwasag sip wali uro tibogalulam. 15 Mog so'o tibalama ane, anap anag agha likto pululneap sembe ya'ag an saeag pimnun. 16 Nari andi nimi yina likto pilulne maikno nembahineba so'o lenglenga saog uro mog so'o to'opto pilul. Andi mangaroba taneko so'o lenglenga saog taleba nimi etneri babe so'o lenglenga a'elbanep kom saog wamleba andi mangaroba taneko babe a'elbhukang kom. 17 Nari mog so'o nene anag pimnundi wene sekmendi mog so'o kemeroa yabululam. Sabo wamla do, kom do?” seog.
18 Wene seogpa, Abram nenekori ae aneko kwerekaogti as Hebron peramag Mamrea kal bari nubu kal wamoghag ae kalun ae sol piogti, Nuni Allah sembe mhong towalengenag sobaog.
